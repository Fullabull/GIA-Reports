European Political Community Summit - Yerevan (May 2026)

Yerevan is currently hosting the European Political Community (EPC) summit, bringing together dozens of world leaders. This explains the sudden surge in aircraft, heavy police presence, road closures, and business shutdowns observed across the city.

What Is Happening

The European Political Community is a large-scale summit involving approximately 45-50 countries. Heads of state, prime ministers, and international organizations are meeting in Yerevan to discuss regional security, economic cooperation, and geopolitical alignment.

This is one of the largest international gatherings ever hosted in Armenia, resulting in full-scale security protocols across the capital.

Why The City Feels Locked Down

  • Dozens of government aircraft arriving within a short window
  • Motorcade routes secured from airport to central Yerevan
  • Temporary closure of roads, businesses, and public areas
  • High-security presence due to the number of global leaders in one location

These conditions are temporary and will normalize once the summit concludes.
