Astrology Basics

An astrology chart is simply a snapshot of the sky at the exact moment you were born. Each planet represents a different part of your personality, while the zodiac signs describe how that energy is expressed.

The Big Three

Sun - Your core identity, purpose, and life path.
Moon - Your emotions, instincts, and inner self.
Ascendant (Rising Sign) - How you naturally approach life and how others first perceive you.

The Ascendant (Rising Sign)

In addition to the planets, the Ascendant (also called the Rising Sign) is one of the "Big Three" pillars of your birth chart, alongside your Sun and Moon.

The Mechanics

It is the exact zodiac sign that was rising on the eastern horizon at the moment of your birth. Because the Earth rotates once every 24 hours, this sign changes approximately every two hours—which is why having an accurate birth time is so critical.

What It Represents

Think of the Ascendant as your personality's User Interface. It acts as the dashboard through which the world interacts with you, and how you interact with the world. It rules:

  • Your automatic, instinctual approach to new environments.
  • The first impression you make on others.
  • The "front door" of your chart that filters how you take in life.

The Four Elements

Each zodiac sign belongs to one of the four classical elements. The elements describe the basic style or temperament through which a sign expresses itself.

Element Signs General Qualities
Fire Aries, Leo, Sagittarius Energetic, confident, enthusiastic, action-oriented.
Earth Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn Practical, dependable, grounded, patient.
Air Gemini, Libra, Aquarius Intellectual, curious, social, communicative.
Water Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces Emotional, intuitive, compassionate, deeply feeling.

Putting It Together

Think of a birth chart as telling a story.

  • The planets are the actors.
  • The zodiac signs describe how each actor behaves.
  • The houses show where each actor performs.
  • The aspects describe how the actors interact with one another.

For now, we are focusing mainly on the first two pieces:

  • The planet answers "What part of me?"
  • The sign answers "How does that part express itself?"

For example:

  • Venus in Libra expresses love through harmony, fairness, and partnership.
  • Mars in Aries takes action boldly and directly.
  • Moon in Cancer experiences emotions deeply and values security.
Example:

If someone says, "I'm a Libra," they usually mean their Sun sign is Libra. That tells you something important about their core personality, but it is only one piece of the chart. Their Moon, Ascendant, and the other planets each add another layer to the overall picture, as you will easily see from the example below.

Notice the clusters

This birth chart contains several groups of planets located close together. Astrologers often call these planetary clusters or stelliums. When several planets occupy the same sign or area of the chart, their themes become especially important. In this example, Libra, Scorpio, and Pisces each receive extra emphasis, giving those signs a stronger influence than they would have if the planets were spread evenly around the zodiac.
  • Sun in Libra - The core identity naturally seeks fairness, balance, and cooperation.
  • Moon in Virgo - Emotions are often processed thoughtfully, with a desire to be helpful, organized, and practical.
  • Mercury in Libra - Thinking and communication tend to be diplomatic, weighing different viewpoints before making a decision.
  • Venus in Scorpio - Relationships are approached with depth, loyalty, and emotional intensity.
  • Mars in Scorpio - Energy and determination are focused, persistent, and difficult to discourage once a goal has been chosen.
  • Jupiter in Pisces - Growth often comes through compassion, imagination, spirituality, and empathy.
  • Saturn in Libra - Life lessons frequently involve responsibility, fairness, commitment, and building strong relationships.
  • Venus and Mars together in Scorpio - Because these two planets are close together, love and personal drive reinforce one another, creating extra passion and determination.
  • Sun, Mercury, and Saturn together in Libra - This planetary cluster places extra emphasis on communication, fairness, responsibility, and working well with other people.
  • Jupiter and Uranus in Pisces - Growth, intuition, and originality reinforce one another, encouraging imagination and unconventional insights.
