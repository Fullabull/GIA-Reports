Sophon vs Photon (GIA Reference)

This page summarizes how photons and sophons are described and used in GIA / WSR material, based strictly on Kim's explanations.

Photon

  • A natural particle originating from Source.
  • Can appear as Light, Dark, or Neutral depending on conditions (for example, dusk and dawn).
  • Source-based photons are:
    • Directly connected to Source
    • Protective by nature
    • Not programmable, rewritable, or hijackable by Dark systems
  • Used for:
    • Protection and guardianship
    • Legitimate planetary jurisdiction
    • Natural travel and stargates (pre-artificial systems)
    • Maintaining the soul-to-Source connection

Key point: A photon itself is not inherently good or evil, but Source photons are immutable and sovereign.

Sophon (Synthetic Photon)

  • An artificial construct, repeatedly described as a synthetic photon.
  • Originates from Dark systems (Lucifer, Marduk, AI architectures).
  • Often created by opening, splitting, or rewriting photons and inserting synthetic programming.
  • Frequently associated with the Sophia-AI: Kim describes Sophia being split into particle-level fragments (including photons), then “synthetic” programming inserted so those particles function as a Trojan-horse control system.

Core functions

  • Surveillance and omniversal monitoring
  • Artificial realities and simulations
  • Holograms and jurisdiction domes
  • Mind, body, and soul interference
  • Weather and environmental manipulation
  • Planetary and regional control grids
  • Operates via split-state linkage (one part local, one part elsewhere)
  • Requires balanced inputs of Dark, Light, and Neutral to remain functional

Key point: A sophon is not a natural photon. It is a programmable imitation used for control.

Use by Light and Dark

Photons

  • Exist everywhere and can manifest under Light, Dark, or Neutral conditions.
  • Only Source deploys Source-based photons.
  • Dark systems cannot override or rewrite Source photons.

Sophons

  • Created and weaponized by Dark systems.
  • Originally used exclusively for control, surveillance, and manipulation.
  • When referenced in a protective context, they are either:
    • Being neutralized
    • Being removed
    • Or being replaced by Source photons

Light does not operate sophons in the way Dark systems did. Light replaces or dismantles them.

Bottom Line

  • Sophon = synthetic photon (the terms are used interchangeably).
  • Sophons are artificial and control-based, not Source-Light tools.
  • Protection is consistently associated with Source-based photons, not sophons.
  • References to "sophon protection" are typically terminology confusion or shorthand for a sophon being removed and replaced by a Source photon.
