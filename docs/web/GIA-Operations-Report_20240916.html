Summary

Kim opens with brief political commentary, noting a second Trump fake assassination attempt and the general disarray on both sides of the U.S. election. She characterizes both candidates as representing dead people and both parties as running on empty, with no coherent direction coming from any faction vying for world dominance.

The centerpiece of the report is an extended disclosure on the Global Intelligence Agency — its origins, structure, and transformation. Kim explains that the GIA was created by Marduk approximately 16,000 years ago as the highest intelligence agency on the planet, above the ACIO, Monarch Military, and all Secret Space Program militaries. Originally comprising around 25–26 million human and non-human members, its mandate was to ensure every downline faction — the 21 Parents, 42 Covens, Coven Masters, Family Masters, and the Black Sun — adhered to Marduk's orders, with disappearance as the consequence for non-compliance. When Kim became Ground Commander on March 31, 2016, she inherited the GIA. Members were given full information and allowed to choose sides; most departed, leaving the current active roster at approximately 170,000 — roughly 70% non-human. The GIA operates by infiltration, cloaking, and consciousness mimicry, maintaining eyes and ears inside Deep State meetings, military installations, and government structures worldwide.

Kim then walks through the mechanism behind blue light manipulation, using an embedded video from an LSU neurosurgeon who traces the program from CIA MK-Ultra experiments at Tulane through casino slot-machine psychology to the current blue-lit screen infrastructure. Kim contextualizes this within her broader framework: the blue light spectrum targets the Mental Plane specifically, allowing the Omega System to suppress independent thought, induce manufactured fears, monitor human consciousness as a data feed, and even attempt disease transmission through frequency. She confirms the program predates the CIA by hundreds of millennia and ran through the Omega System, which she states will be fully severed by September 18th. She recommends blue light glasses as a practical countermeasure until then.

Kim addresses the annual Harvest Moon Family meetings concluding the following day, noting that operatives are expecting contracts that will not materialize. Tom Melville fielded approximately five hours of questions from meeting participants trying to understand how Kim's systems work — questions Kim read as confirmation that they have not found another human capable of doing what she does and are growing desperate. She also confirms the Mount Denali SSP operation over the weekend was shut down.

The report closes with an extended segment on upcoming UNN format changes: GIA files (standalone deep-dive videos on specific topics), expanded Field Messenger coverage, a restructured newscast separating operational intelligence from background education, and a marketplace beta launch targeted for November 1st. Kim also explains the essential oils program — describing it as a Source-aligned counterpart to spectral manipulation, using terpene science and Rife machine frequencies to deliver healing energy through a controlled medium. In response to Sunny's question about SSP time travel, Kim confirms that the ability to move human consciousness across timelines without sovereign consent is ending along with the rest of the Omega System's access.
