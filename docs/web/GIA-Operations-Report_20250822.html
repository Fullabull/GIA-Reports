Summary

Kim reports on the rare decennial meeting of the Covens, Parents, and Ancients, coinciding with the Black Moon and Equinox period. With the absence of Marduk, Enki, Enlil, and the Parents, the Ancients attempt to run the meeting but face major losses in power and authority. She details the collapse of the dimensional control grid, failed attempts to reinfect humanity with parasites, and the inability of the Deep State and Ancients to appoint new leaders. Kim emphasizes that Source has cut off their access, leaving them powerless, and suggests that the war is essentially won, with further updates expected after the weekend meetings.
