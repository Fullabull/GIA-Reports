Summary

Kim reflects on the Deep State's increasing desperation, highlighting how General McCaffrey's failed attack on her led to disastrous results for the Deep State. She explains the turmoil among Deep State operatives as their financial system continues to collapse, with Israel declaring a ceasefire against Lebanon. Kim also discusses the struggle for control over the **Greater Israel Project** and reaffirms her commitment to maintaining stability by rejecting corruption, despite the pressure from global elites. Kim closes with a Thanksgiving reflection on her work and gratitude for the support she's received.

Table of Contents

  • General McCaffrey's failed attack on Kim, resulting in loss of equipment and backlash.
  • Israel declares a 60-day ceasefire against Lebanon, tied to Deep State influence over global politics.
  • Deep State operatives' continuing efforts to control resources, financial systems, and military power.
  • Revelation of the **Greater Israel Project** and its ties to war, genocide, and global financial manipulation.
  • Thanksgiving reflection from Kim on her progress, gratitude for the channel's supporters, and the upcoming **New Earth Innovations** series.
  • Kim's call for transparency, the need for community-led governance, and a focus on Source-driven restructuring.
  • The ongoing struggle between Source-aligned systems and the Deep State's failing agendas.
