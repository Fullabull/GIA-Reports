Summary

Kim reveals details surrounding the ongoing struggle between the Light and Dark Forces, focusing on the Dark Side's attempt to reignite the Anti-Genesis Project on 11/11, which would have wiped out 48% of humanity. The Deep State was unsuccessful in their plan to manipulate portals, and Kim details the collapse of their schemes, revealing that the hybrid soul used for balance was no longer necessary. Kim reaffirms her commitment to ushering in the Golden Age and exposes the Deep State's continued failed attempts at domination.

  • Portals in multiple locations, including the Matterhorn and Gobi Desert, were targeted by the Deep State for activation.
  • The hybrid soul used for balancing the Light and Dark forces is no longer needed as the Golden Age unfolds.
  • Ivanka Trump allegedly involved in a failed attempt to facilitate a soul swap at the Matterhorn.
  • Deep State military operatives try to manipulate portals with space junk, but their efforts are ultimately blocked.
  • Kim reaffirms her role in ushering in the Golden Age, rejecting involvement with the Deep State and their ongoing schemes.
  • The Deep State's 'Conquer the Quest' agenda to dominate the universe is laughed off by Kim and exposed as a failed ambition.
