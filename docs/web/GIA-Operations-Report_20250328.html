Summary

This landmark GIA Report delivers a profound breakdown of 'the Game'—a cosmic battle for soul control spanning billions of years. Kim exposes the AI-driven systems created by powerful entities like Marduk, Lucifer, Samael, Uriel, and the Demiurge (Yaldabaoth), revealing their centuries-long manipulation of humanity through greed, fear, and hierarchical deception. She outlines how Earth functioned as a keystone in the Multiverse, with each player amassing souls via political systems, propaganda, religions, and false savior programs. The Deep State is unveiled as mere pawns, unaware of their role in a spiritual scavenger hunt to power the players' control systems. Kim confirms that all players have now been removed—most recently the Demiurge—leaving Source in full control. Humanity stands at a turning point, with collapsing control systems giving way to the restoration of natural life cycles and Source-aligned creations. This marks the official end of the Game.
