Summary

Kim opens by addressing the Deep State's expectations around Halloween and the closing of the Lions Gate alignment — the last day when Source, Anti-Source, and Neutral Source align. The Deep State believed the New Moon of November 1st would trigger another such reversal. Kim discloses that residual connections to the being known as Kali Ma were found and eliminated in China, the Middle East, and the United States — with thanks to the Enforcer — before ceremonies could begin. Kim raises the Ways and Means Committee as a private NGO that could potentially fund Social Security, Medicare, and public education directly, bypassing the federal government entirely. She closes by confirming the Golden Age transition is sealed for at least 2,000 years, and provides brief updates on the C.A.R.E. Marketplace launch and New Earth Innovations products expected within two weeks.
