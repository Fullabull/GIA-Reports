Philip K. Dick - Films and TV Lineage

This page lists movies and television shows that are either direct adaptations of Philip K. Dick's work or are widely recognized as thematic descendants influenced by his ideas.

Direct Film Adaptations

  • Blade Runner (1982) - based on Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep?
  • Blade Runner 2049 (2017) - sequel set in the same universe
  • Total Recall (1990, 2012) - based on We Can Remember It for You Wholesale
  • Minority Report (2002) - based on The Minority Report
  • A Scanner Darkly (2006) - based on A Scanner Darkly
  • Paycheck (2003) - based on Paycheck
  • Impostor (2001) - based on Impostor
  • Next (2007) - based on The Golden Man
  • The Adjustment Bureau (2011) - based on Adjustment Team
  • Radio Free Albemuth (2010) - based on Radio Free Albemuth
  • Confessions d'un Barjo (1992) - based on Confessions of a Crap Artist

Television Adaptations

  • The Man in the High Castle (2015-2019)
  • Philip K. Dick's Electric Dreams (2017-2018) - anthology series

Thematic and Unofficial Descendants

These works are not direct adaptations but strongly reflect Philip K. Dick's recurring themes: identity, false reality, memory manipulation, artificial consciousness, and control systems.

  • Dark City (1998)
  • The Matrix Trilogy (1999-2021)
  • Inception (2010)
  • eXistenZ (1999)
  • Vanilla Sky (2001)
  • Westworld (TV, 2016-2022)
