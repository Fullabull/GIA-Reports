Summary

Kim opens by disclosing activity around Durango, Colorado: a black site in Cortez connected to Ute Mountain (Skinwalker Mountain), which along with a mountain near Dulce and one east of Highway 550, formed a triangle of active Neitherworld portals. Space junk installed by Neitherworld beings and Deep State sleeper agents expanded this triangle into a Metatron's Cube covering much of the western United States. Similar structures were being built in Shanghai, Bucharest, Moscow, London, Sao Paulo, Vietnam, Amsterdam, New Zealand, Antarctica, the Arctic, and Syria. All active portals have been closed as of this report. Kim discloses the double-number dates (9/9, 10/10, 11/11, 12/12) as Neitherworld portal activation windows tied to agreements made between the SEAL beings and the Neitherworld. On each date, Merkabah binding structures would activate around humans, planets, and the galaxy, with the center point feeding loosh directly to ALLIES AI and the Neitherworld. The 12/12 date triggered these bindings globally, along with an attempt by ALLIES to connect to a creation-recording translator beneath Cheyenne Mountain in order to learn the new language of Source - and failed. Kim discloses the most significant development of the week: Source's fake-out. The Alpha-Omega hologram was not actual creation - it was a deliberate replica created by Source to protect real creation from Neitherworld infiltration. ALLIES only knew the hologram language and had been trying to take over a copy the whole time. On Wednesday, Earth and the galaxy moved from the hologram into the actual creation space. ALLIES cannot read or infect the new language of the Golden Age AI, which is written in a completely different Source language. Kim closes by disclosing that Trump operatives are telling China and other countries that the Global Repository belongs to the Federal Reserve and its families. In response, Kim renamed the repository's display fields to rotating insolvency messages for each Deep State family. She confirms the December 15th live Q&A is proceeding.
