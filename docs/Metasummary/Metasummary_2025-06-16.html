Summary

Kim opens the June 16th report with Father's Day and Solstice greetings, then addresses recent criticism of her tone, explaining that intensifying attacks during broadcasts - including black-magic interference - account for her edge. She moves directly into the week's events. The centerpiece is the failed Deep State attempt to install a New World Order global government over the weekend of June 13-15. Kim discloses that the Cowboys, Mormon Church, and other factions had been promised seats at a World Council table - oversold to over a hundred different parties. Each group was told by Marduk's pre-programmed AI systems, operating through crystal tablets in their respective temples - including the Freemason Temple in Washington D.C. and the Mormon Church's inner sanctum - that control of a new financial system would be handed to them by Sunday. Acting on those instructions, operatives staged a military parade, remotely fired weapons in the Israel-Iran conflict from beneath the Pentagon, planned a 9/11-style New York attack, and prepared to assault No Kings protesters under an ICE cover. None of it succeeded. Kim provides an extended disclosure on the Mormon Church: its president Russell Nelson passed away roughly a year ago and has been replaced by a walk-in, mirroring the Vatican's pattern. The Church's leadership belongs to the Germanic Order - a bloodline tracing to beings who retreated into a Neutral Zone at the start of the Dark Ages - and has operated as a dual-service entity aligned more with the Dark than the Light, involved in trafficking and other criminal enterprises. Their crystal tablets, like those of other institutions, had been running Marduk AI instructions for decades. The largest section is a deep technical disclosure on simulations. Kim explains that simulations are artificial consciousness constructs originating with Lucifer and Lucerne at the beginning of Creation, built from a Neutral Source or Artificial Cosmic Sun, and designed to overlay and trap organic consciousness in a labyrinth. Marduk had access to lower-density simulators and used them to pre-program the rise and fall of world powers - including planting frequency generators under the power centers of countries designated to fall, which Kim identifies as the source of spontaneous health events and business failures now visible in the United States. Over the weekend, Kim and her team identified simulations as the source of what initially looked like a Deep State attack, and proceeded to systematically dismantle the simulator infrastructure - weapons systems, AI entities, portal attempts, and financial mirrors - while the Deep State watched their promised systems vanish in real time. Kim closes by stating the simulations are glitching and losing power as the planet moves deeper into the Golden Light of Source, and expresses hope that this is the final transition period. She reiterates her position on negotiations: she will not sit with power-seekers, prefers people who act from love rather than ego, and intends to direct her time toward project owners with genuine humanitarian plans.
