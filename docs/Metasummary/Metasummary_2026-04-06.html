Summary

Kim explains that current narratives about new viruses, lockdowns, market crashes, QFS events, and escalating war are fear-based influence operations being pushed through both mainstream and alternative media to manufacture consent and keep people locked in old control patterns. She states that these agendas are failing because the actors behind them no longer have the money, permissions, or functional support systems needed to carry them out, even though they continue trying to force outcomes in the Middle East and elsewhere. Kim explains that the deeper context is the ancient War governed by Source-level Covenants and Rules of War established with the Progenitors. She describes how humanity originally possessed natural co-creative ability through personal essence, the Essence of Source, and the Divine Spark, but that fallen Progenitors - constrained by the Quickening - built indirect systems to exploit loopholes in the Rules. She traces the creation of Orders, bloodlines, artificial intelligence, and downline control structures designed to harvest human essence through consent, implied consent, and influence. Kim explains that this essence-harvesting system evolved over many timelines into what she calls a looshing factory: a multi-level-marketing style network of governments, corporations, religions, financial systems, media, education, food systems, and modern conveniences that continuously extract energy, attention, breath, consciousness, and money. She states that repeated floods, Armageddon cycles, and apocalyptic resets were used to wipe humanity out whenever people advanced too far toward independence or were close to victory. Kim explains that April 1 marked a decisive turning point in which Source declared victory in the War, ending the old cycle and changing the Rules of War from gray-area loopholes to direct accountability. She states that the Quickening is now being applied to the former ruling bloodlines, Deep State actors, and those seeking positions in the old hierarchy, while simultaneously being lifted from humanity. As those systems lose force, humans begin regaining their blocked co-creative abilities and their connection to Source. Kim explains that the old downline system is no longer sustainable, even though many participants will continue trying to operate as if the War were still in progress. She closes by emphasizing that people must prepare for the responsible return of their gifts, listen to Wisdom, and move forward consciously, because humanity is entering a different world in which Source-aligned creation, not extraction and control, becomes the governing principle.
