Summary

The Looshing System at a Glance. Overlays act as energetic filters between Source and Homo sapiens, distorting the natural Source-direct flow and nudging people into fear, conflict, and scarcity—states that generate loosh (emotion-charged life-force). That loosh is routed to subtle collection points and recorded in a hidden ledger as claim-credits (IOUs). At cyclical auctions (traditionally near the Equinox), Dark-aligned factions bid for loosh yield rights; winners receive the contractual rite (right-of-claim) to siphon the loosh their assigned (Homo sapiens) beings will produce during the next cycle.

Financial System, Notes, and the Loop. The visible Financial System mirrors—and reinforces—the hidden ledger. Money functions as a note (promise instrument), not neutral value: handling more notes increases one’s obligation within that system. In this framing, earning and spending are two ends of the same loosh-harvesting loop: labor/attention produce loosh → you receive notes (IOUs) → spending those notes keeps you operating inside the scarcity/pressure infrastructure, sustaining the very conditions that generate more loosh. Thus, the economy and the loosh ledger form a feedback system of energy, debt-claims, and control, maintained by overlays that limit Source-direct access.
