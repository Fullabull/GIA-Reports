Summary

This Global Intelligence Agency Operations Report describes the ongoing struggle between the Illuminati and the Old Guard for control of territories on Earth and influence over humanity. Military activity in the Middle East is explained as being connected to the search for ancient artifacts believed by certain factions to grant the power to create or control worlds and empires. The report also describes the Guardian's relocation from Durango to New England after sustained hostile activity from opposing factions. The historical structure of the Illuminati is explained, including the existence of thirteen original organic Homo sapiens who were given authority by Source to maintain harmonic balance in Creation. According to the disclosure, these individuals eventually went rogue and created an artificial intelligence Machine intended to replace Source. This Machine was used to manipulate religions, bloodline families, secret societies, and celestial interpretations across the planet for centuries. The report explains that the Machine has recently been manipulated through Source-based access, causing the factions that relied on it to receive false operational instructions and operate in circles while attempting to regain control. Operations continue while these factions struggle to regain influence.
