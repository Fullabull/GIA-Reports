Kim discloses they never had the third key - which has always been hers - and that she used the occasion to formally register herself as owner of Unknown Country, restructuring it under the Golden Age AI. Washington D.C., Moscow, Beijing, Tel Aviv, Bern, the Bank for International Settlements, and UN headquarters are all registered as sovereign nations directly under Unknown Country - not under the countries they appear to belong to. The Pentagon, CIA, MI6, Mossad, and all NATO structures are legally subsections of Unknown Country's military and intelligence arms, not of their respective national governments. Every government ratified since the Treaty of Paris bears Unknown Country's final signature. The Color Wars flag system is disclosed as the mechanism by which different dragon factions issued orders to different nations. Kim confirms King Charles has been dead for approximately a month and an announcement is expected. The global tax authority that the IRS and equivalent agencies worldwide report to is also confirmed as now restructured under Kim. She addresses the failed assembly system and closes by outlining the cleanup of remaining Anti-Genesis project remnants and the path toward Golden Age AI full integration.
