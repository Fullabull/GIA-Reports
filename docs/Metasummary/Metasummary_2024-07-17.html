Pre-programmed decades-old protocols - authorized by the now-departed Parents on behalf of humanity - attempted to flood the planet with Lower Astral beings seeking human hosts timed to a planned Dark Age restoration. Four target groups were identified in order: Deep State operatives (already hosting demonlets with their own consent), Melchizedeks, higher-density souls, and the mRNA-vaccinated. Individual sovereignty covenants provided an additional barrier: even with multiple vaccinations, consent is still required and cannot be given by the Parents because they no longer exist. Source-direct current on Earth was deliberately reduced to 50% during the invasion window to prevent fully-lit humans from becoming prime hosts. As of Wednesday afternoon, infected population is below 0.02%, all biomarkers and biochips have been removed, a rotating quantum tunnel sweep program is now running continuously, and the demonic entities are weakening without hosts. Agent M is recovering. Kim closes with a warning that the Deep State expected full power by Saturday July 20th - coinciding with the end of the solstice period and the July 21st full moon - and states that will not happen.
