Summary

Kim opens by disclosing what was actually happening at Mar-a-Lago on November 21st. Clone One of Trump began malfunctioning due to a soul tether severing connected to the 11/11-11/22 portal closing. The SSP had planned to stage a death or assassination on 11/22 - deliberately mirroring the JFK date - to use as a martyr narrative and exit strategy from the mountain of broken promises made to operatives, media figures, and foreign governments. The plan backfired when Clone One actually began failing on its own. Clone Two was brought in as a replacement, but being a more synthetic hybrid, malfunctioned in an overtly inhuman way, became violent, and had to be put down. Kim notes that multiple other elite clones worldwide lost their tethers through the same portal event, and expects more to expire within 72 hours. Kim delivers a whiteboard briefing on the true nature of the 11/11 portal. It is not a connection to Source or the Creator but a point where the Nil - the zero point between the Light Side Alphaverse and the Dark Side Omegaverse - briefly opens. During this window, guardians and gatekeepers of the densities would battle for control of the access points. Densities 1-3 were monitored by Tarzakyans and Targethyans; Densities 4-6 by Artemis's reptilian army and Baal replicas; Densities 7-9 by Astaroth on behalf of Anti-Source. This year's portal was fully activated - unusual, and likely because humanity crossed the Light threshold. Kim discloses that last year's legions that appeared on Earth around November 13th were actually fulfilling this gatekeeper battle role on behalf of Source. This year there were no opposing beings, only space junk at portal sites, which was cleared. The severing of clone tethers was a side effect of this process. Kim closes with updates on the upcoming December 15th live Q&A and continued C.A.R.E. Marketplace beta testing.
