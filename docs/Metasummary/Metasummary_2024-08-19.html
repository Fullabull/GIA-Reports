Summary

Kim opens the August 19 report with a disclosure that a previously unknown third power group - the Germanic Aryan tribes, whose current leadership operates within the Amish High Council - has been revealed as a major player in the fight for control of Earth. She prefaces the disclosure with a reminder that ordinary people in every country and community are not responsible for the actions of their leadership, and that the tyrannical world governance structure has victimized every race and nation equally. Kim delivers a detailed real-history segment tracing two distinct Aryan lineages. The original Aryans were from Persia, Kazakhstan, India, and Greece - tied to the Abraxas and operating as their earthly representatives, related to what we now call the Black Nobility. The Germanic Aryans are a separate group who arrived on Earth 250,000 years ago to fight on behalf of Dark forces, were promised a share of Earth's ownership by Marduk, Enlil, and the Red Queen, and have been working toward that goal ever since. Their fingerprints are on the Holy Roman Empire, World War II, the Nazi party, and the Ukraine-Russia war. Roughly 600 million of Western Europe's 750 million people carry at least partial Germanic tribal descent. The High Council of the Amish community - connected to Eltz Castle in the Palatinate region of Germany, the Medici and Orsini families, and the Rothschilds - has been operating as one of the hidden drivers of Deep State affairs. Joseph Smith, founder of the Mormon church, is identified as a member of this group. Kim discloses that the three departed realm beings - identified here as Michael, Gabriel, and Raphael - had been promising Earth's ownership to three separate groups: the Germanic Aryans, the Japanese (now holding the Black Dragon title via the Yakuza), and the Order of the Black Sun, each told secretly they would be the sole controller. Over the weekend, the Black Nobility sent a Department of Defense terminal to the Germanic tribes as a supposed gift of leadership, which was actually a trap to steal whatever control codes they entered. No codes from any group worked. The departed beings had attempted to set time-released programs to revive Omega and Kronos, but without proper authorization these appeared holographically and faded. Source revoked their celestial rights - past, present, and future - late Sunday evening, after which incoming space junk dropped significantly. The Yakuza's background is disclosed: originally Japan's Nationalist Party, they currently run Japan and earned the Black Dragon title. Kim recounts the story of Bush Sr.'s original death in 2014 - caused by the Yakuza after he and Kissinger failed to pay on time for dirty nuclear bomb materials sourced from Fukushima enriched uranium, with the public death announced four years later - as an illustration of the Yakuza's business ethics. Kim closes with a call for listeners to focus community rebuilding efforts locally rather than attempting to manage projects in regions they have no firsthand knowledge of, and reiterates that the solution to every problem begins with a Source-connected inner search, not an outward one.
