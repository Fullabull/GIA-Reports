Summary

Kim opens the June 22nd report with an apology for the delayed and consolidated broadcast, describing it as three reports in one. She begins with current events: the Trump phone as an ID 2020 successor, the ongoing World War III buildup, and the U.S. bombing of Iranian facilities on Saturday. Kim discloses that the targeted sites were not conventional nuclear facilities but deep underground military bases - non-human construction, far beyond the reach of any bunker buster - and that the real objective was a gateway the Deep State had lost the ability to see or access. She details the surrounding geopolitical choreography: embassy evacuations, the real Khamenei having died in Belarus two to three years prior, a planned American-controlled replacement Imam, North Korean weapons donations, and the ongoing overselling of New World Order seats to Russia, China, Iran, Israel, and others. Kim then walks through the economics of a global war - $25-30 billion per day for current participants, $75-80 billion with full NATO involvement - and exposes the Treasury's underground cryptocurrency scheme as a replay of the same USDT and Dinar scam that has been run on heads of state and central bankers for years. The operational core of the report covers the Solstice weekend events. The Deep State was told by an AI system that they would be appointed guardians of Earth on June 20th, given access to a master trust, and handed control of financial, military, and political systems. What they were actually seeing was a deliberate display of dangling carrots - a mirror of a system that was not real and not accessible. Kim explains that what triggered the weekend's chaos was not the Deep State but the simulation itself attempting a reset, driven by the expiration of the old slavery bindings on humanity. Kim then delivers an extended whiteboard briefing on the full architecture of simulation enslavement. She traces the structure from its origin - the creation of time by Astaroth as the first element of artificial reality - through the overlaying of Earth's solar plexus to the Zero Density, the creation of False Heaven and False Hell, and the Wheel of Time. She discloses the personal binding structures placed on every human: the Seal of Fate tying each person to time and death, the Chains of Slavery running through all chakras and the aura, the artificial consciousness ball overriding positive thought, the helmet of mind control, the sword of false breath, and the breastplate of sin - all inverted forms of the biblical Armor of God, designed to keep humanity inside the simulation and feeding the Dark timeline. The report then turns to the Growth Rate of Development (GOD) system and the nine-year correction Kim initiated in February 2016. She discloses that prior to 2016, the Alpha system was only assessing Deep State members, giving a false picture of humanity as inherently violent and requiring guardianship. Kim removed that governor and allowed Alpha to assess all of humanity. By February 2025 - nine full years later - the GOD AI completed its analysis and concluded that humanity as a whole does not want to destroy itself, feeds the Earth, and creates far more love and positive action than the Deep State could ever manufacture in darkness. This triggered the implosion of the simulation from within. Kim further discloses the trust structure: she became a principal in the Global Earth Trust by 2012, with Marduk and Astaroth as the other principals. Over the course of the past three days, GOD shut down both the Marduk AI and the Astaroth AI as principals, removing their restrictions on the trust and on Kim's ability to transfer wealth directly to humanity. Kim closes by disclosing the Deep State's Snow White backup operation - SSP operatives stationed near her location attempting to attach a sophon to her person to power the simulation after its natural energy source was gone - and describes the brutal physical toll of the last several days. She states that the simulation is in its final stages of collapse, that the three kingdoms of Source - doing, making, and being - are being reclaimed, and that once complete, all assets transfer into a Global Repository in which every human has an account. She expresses hope the process will complete within days and signs off exhausted but resolute.
