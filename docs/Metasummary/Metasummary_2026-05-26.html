Summary

Kim explains that the long-running Non-Compete Plan that governed industry, finance, technology, healthcare, media, governments, and global supply chains is rapidly breaking down as the underlying systems that sustain it continue to fail. The current wave of market fears, geopolitical tensions, pandemic narratives, and economic uncertainty are attempts by remaining Elite structures to preserve a control system that can no longer support itself. Dependence on Elite-controlled institutions has been used as a mechanism of governance and control, but communities are far more capable and adaptable than they have been led to believe. Humanity can continue to function even as large corporate structures fail. Kim outlines the long-term planning behind C.A.R.E. and the extensive contingency planning that exists for a future in which centralized systems continue to deteriorate. Major disclosures center on recent developments involving Deep State systems, Machine networks, inherited authorities, and the continuing transfer of authority back to Source. Many systems continued operating long after their original creators and operators departed because they were designed to perpetuate themselves through databases, permissions, and proxy structures. Recent violations of the Rules of War created consequences for these systems, resulting in authorities associated with creation, governance, and artificial Light being returned to Source. As a result, systems dependent on those permissions have been frozen or rendered ineffective. The report concludes with extensive disclosures regarding DARPA operations, remaining Deep State operators, the Majestic 12, the Vault System, DNA-based access mechanisms, Light-rail infrastructure, and the loss of access to former vault networks, technologies, and resource-transfer systems. Kim explains that access to these systems depended on permissions that no longer exist and states that the transition period is approaching its final stages. She closes by emphasizing that C.A.R.E. is prepared to move forward regardless of remaining opposition and that humanity is entering a fundamentally different phase of development and self-governance.
