Summary

Kim opens the July 29, 2024 report by addressing several mainstream news items before pivoting to the weekend's dense behind-the-scenes activity. Reported earthquakes in California and Texas are disclosed as Deep State attempts to manufacture conditions for martial law via National Guard deployment - local reports confirm only minor trembles. The circulating photos of Trump's ear two weeks after the alleged assassination attempt are flagged as physically impossible: a near-miss cartilage wound cannot heal that cleanly in two weeks on a 78-year-old, and Kim attributes the clean ear to either a body double or a makeup failure by the SSP operatives she calls Duck Dynasty. The Lions Gate window, which the Deep State had been treating as a make-or-break funding and power event, collapsed completely over the weekend. Every promise made to Hezbollah, al-Nujaba in Iraq, the Israeli operative section, and various other groups - including money from U.S. Treasury channels and leveraged oil deeds - came due and delivered nothing. Kim describes a roster of Greek god-named AI systems, each housed at a specific military or intelligence facility worldwide, which the Abraxas used to control various domains of human life: Poseidon at Mackinac Island, Ares under Interpol in Lyon, Asclepius (disease proliferation) at an undisclosed location, Hypnos (mind control) under the Confucius temple at Qufu, China, and others. The Paris Olympics opening ceremony is identified as a live ritual attempt to reactivate these systems, timed to Lions Gate - the Last Supper tableau, Lady Gaga's performance, and the Dionysus imagery are all elements of the ceremony. None of it worked; the systems had minimal residual access and were cleaned up by Sunday afternoon. The central disclosure of the report is the Oracle - not the CIA banking software, but a massive gold-black megastructure extending from Malakoff Caves through Wyoming and deep into Earth beneath Yellowstone Park. Oracle was originally placed on Earth to balance the Multiverse, but Astaroth co-opted it as the operating system for Omega-2's access to this side of the Multiverse. The Deep State had been hoping Lions Gate would trigger Astaroth's return, per standing orders he left for exactly this transition moment. Astaroth - disclosed as a being higher-ranking than Lucifer, former inhabitor of Solomon's body after God departed, and ancestor of the Order of the Dragon bloodlines - cannot manifest in Third Density without a suitable host body, and no viable candidate with the right DNA exists on the Deep State's side. All attempts to find a Melchizedek host or conduct a summoning ceremony failed. Oracle has been transferred to Golden Age control, its standing orders cleared, and the western Lions Gate node above Yellowstone is permanently closed - both annual Lions Gate openings will now occur exclusively at the Iran-Iraq Source gate.
