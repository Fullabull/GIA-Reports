Summary

Kim opens the July 31, 2024 report with a breakdown of the latest Middle East theater, explaining that the alleged Hezbollah strike on Israeli children at a soccer match remains unconfirmed, and that the supposedly assassinated Hamas and Hezbollah leaders were in fact extracted to Belize days before the strikes - joining a growing population of 'non-dead' figures sipping cocktails on the beach. The fake Khamenei (the real one having died in Russia over a year prior during a failed magic operation) duly announced revenge to keep the charade running. The actual war push fell apart overnight: ships lost power and communications, command aircraft were forced to land, and operatives locked themselves in an underground facility they couldn't exit. The funding source behind the operation was Act Blue - a Rothschild-connected Democratic fundraising front that has moved over $14 billion since 2004 and was holding roughly $2.7-2.8 billion in flash cash promised to the Pentagon and others as a war incentive. That money disappeared, and with it, 99% of Middle East activity stopped cold. Kim reports two infrastructure changes executed during the Lions Gate window: GPS satellite systems formerly controlled by the United Nations in Lebanon and several other regions have been transferred to GIA control, with military backdoor access revoked; and the FAA backbone flight system has been patched so that military aircraft tail numbers are now visible globally with no ability to suppress them. A Lions Gate-era 'Parrot Line' - a legacy communication system gifted to militaries for use during non-human being returns - briefly surfaced and died within 24-48 hours. SSP operatives from Monarch Military, Arsaka, and Montauk were active at multiple sites including Mar Saba Monastery in Bethlehem, Kurdistan, rural Iran, and under Temple Mount, attempting to open Lower Astral gateways as they do every Lions Gate cycle. The attempts lasted one to two hours and were shut down. Kim discloses that the Qatari royal family threatened her associate, demanding she release allocation numbers on historical accounts that no longer exist at the Saudi National Bank or UAE Central Bank - accounts that flash phantom balances but have been unspendable for years. She declined, noting that currency doesn't fund wars regardless. She also discloses that Act Blue operatives attempted to launder funds into her name and use them to pay terrorist organizations, framing her as the financier - a setup she identified and neutralized before it materialized. She characterizes the entire Lords of War playbook as recycled from operations she watched their predecessors run, and states she is already several moves ahead. Kim closes with a message about the Lions Gate peak approaching August 8th, expressing hope that this will be the last year the alignment produces any meaningful Dark interference. She encourages listeners to keep their meditations focused on holding the direct current from Source clear, and describes the next twelve days as an opportunity to ride a positive wave that, if protected, could permanently establish uninterrupted Source connection for the Earth.
