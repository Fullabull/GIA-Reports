Summary

Kim reports that the U.S. government has temporarily reopened using blackmail based on the Epstein files, shaking loose large donations from wealthy Middle Eastern families while pretending it is about exposing pedophiles and human traffickers. Behind the scenes, however, the true story is the collapse of color coded empires that once spanned the Omniverse, controlled by layered AI emperors and managed through royal Dragon Families, Secret Space Programs, and Deep State scriptwriters based in facilities like the 7th Floor of the U.S. State Department and the 13th Floor at HSBC Hong Kong. Over the past week, including a brutal Sunday night and Monday battle, Kim and Source have dismantled the emperor AIs and converted every empire they tore down into an Empire of Source, reclaiming control of Earth, her Central Sun, and the Sols of Homo sapiens. A 2018 offer to nationalize Washington, D.C. and the Federal Reserve for Trump was long ago declined, and new attempts by the Templars and Red empire operatives to obtain a Covenant have been refused. Deep State factions, Secret Space Program operatives, and military and intelligence personnel who served empires are now cut off from their empire covenants and systems like Langley 5, and face a stark choice to continue raising the dead or stand down as Homo sapiens reclaim their inheritance and walk as sovereign citizens of the Empire of Source.
