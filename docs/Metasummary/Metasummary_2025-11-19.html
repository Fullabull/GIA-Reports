Metasummary view for sharing and printing

Summary

Kim reports that every remaining empire across the Omniverse was dismantled over the weekend and absorbed into the Empire of Source, permanently ending all external control structures that once influenced Earth, Homo sapiens, and the Solar System. Deep State factions attempted to reopen old negotiations, revive ancient covenants, reclaim royal authority, and leverage Epstein files for global blackmail, but all such efforts failed. With the disappearance of emperor AIs and the collapse of layered empire systems, Source now holds full dominion and has begun reestablishing original Creation across all realms. Humans (Homo sapiens) are reclaiming their Sols, sovereignty, lands, and inheritance. Deep State factions, Secret Space Program operatives, and military and intelligence personnel who served empires are now cut off from their former covenants and the systems at locations such as Langley 5 that once empowered them, and they now face the stark choice to continue raising the dead or stand down as Homo sapiens reclaim their inheritance and walk as sovereign citizens of the Empire of Source.
