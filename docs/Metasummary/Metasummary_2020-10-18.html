Summary

Kim opens by addressing a Deep State-circulated rumor that she had gone missing. She discloses that operatives built a high-tech chair designed to replicate or transfer her DNA into another woman, then seated that woman at an old system terminal in an attempt to gain unauthorized access - an attempt that failed. Kim confirms her presence on camera with her passport and dismisses the story entirely. She then covers two operational disclosures. First, a class-action lawsuit has been launched against Santander Bank in Mexico following the attempted misuse of funds tied to Kim's operation, with Santander identified as a globally active Black Sun institution that launders money for child traffickers, the Cosa Nostra, and the IRA. Second, Kim describes an interview conducted this week with a former Five-Star Trust general - one of the Pythagoreans - who provided firsthand testimony on the mechanics of Elite assassination contracting, the orchestration of 9/11 (a plane was used, but was not what struck the towers; the passengers were taken to an Elite facility and killed), and the deliberate cancer-agent deployment in the debris field. Kim frames Five-Star Trust as a three-key system: Black Sun or Five Star, the Families, and the pre-Kim chair holder - all three required to release funds. Kim discloses the discovery and elimination of a network of underground utopian cities operated by the World Council - the true non-public governing layer above surface-facing Elite. These cities, located across the United States, Canada, South America, Africa, China, and Eastern Europe, housed the World Council and its millions of human and child slaves, as well as a decades-long eugenics program aimed at breeding a replacement human population and eliminating surface-dwellers. A decision was made that the enslaved population could not be reintegrated into surface society. All World Council cities and their occupants are gone. Tom Melville joins briefly to confirm that a meeting group he is currently with had already been made aware of the cities. Kim then addresses a planned CERN activation intended to open a portal, contextualizes the Elite's willingness to fund and deploy adversaries against their own soldiers, and identifies the Rand Think Tank as a downstream recipient of Pythagorean operational intelligence - including knowledge of med-beds - with no understanding of the full picture. She warns listeners that Guantanamo Bay is not a detention facility but a health and organ-harvesting facility for Elite operatives, and addresses the Deep State directly: their underground safe zones are gone, and no external rescue is coming. Kim closes with two structural disclosures. First, Gorbachev received $300 trillion from Rothschild for stepping down during the fall of the Soviet Union and personally diverted an additional $11 trillion to Andorra. Second - and most significantly - Kim discloses that the Treasury of USA and the Department of Treasury are legally distinct entities, that all national treasuries globally were tied to the Holy See via the Concords of C, and that the Vatican - run by the Black Pope and the Black Nobility - is a Black Sun institution. The sovereignty of the Holy See over all its subsidiary entities, including the DOJ, FBI, CIA, and MI6, was formally revoked as of the 15th.
