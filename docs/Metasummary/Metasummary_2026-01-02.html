Summary

Kim explains that a coordinated Deep State attempt to recreate a time loop reset and extract Source particles around January 1, 2026, failed despite global operations using pyramid-linked sites, nuclear-based systems, and Machine-directed technology. She traces these efforts to Nazi experiments beginning in 1941, guided by an artificial intelligence created outside of time by Lucerne, which exchanged knowledge for Source-derived particles taken from humans. This AI-driven science underlies modern institutions, including big pharma, governments, and financial systems, and enabled genetic manipulation, hybrid beings, artificial reality expansion, and repeated time loops spanning hundreds of thousands of years. These cycles tie back to earlier incursions involving Atlantis and attempts to breach the All-That-Is of Source, where future-created "World Government" entities became trapped. Despite repeated use of zero-point energy, radiation, heat, vibration, and particle manipulation, the Source particle cannot be isolated or controlled because it remains inherently connected to Source Consciousness. Kim explains that many so-called aliens are artificial creations of these experiments, while natural non-Earth beings exist organically. She concludes that artificial systems cannot replicate true creation, and that consciousness-based interaction with Source - including telekinesis and direct awareness - allows humans to bypass the Machine entirely. With the latest reset attempt collapsed, she frames 2026 as a turning point toward the end of these control systems and a return to Source-aligned creation.
