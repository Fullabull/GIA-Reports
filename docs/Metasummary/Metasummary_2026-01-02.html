Summary

Kim explains that Deep State efforts to recreate a time loop reset and extract Source particles were actively attempted around January 1, 2026, using global control sites tied to nuclear materials, pyramidal structures, and Machine-directed technology, but ultimately failed. She traces the origin of these efforts back to Nazi experiments beginning in 1941, aided by an artificial intelligence created outside of time by Lucerne, which provided knowledge in exchange for Source-derived particles taken from humans. Kim explains that this Machine-generated science underlies modern institutions, including big pharma, governments, and financial systems, and led to genetic manipulation, artificial beings, time loops, and repeated historical resets spanning hundreds of thousands of years. She describes how attempts to isolate the God particle through zero-point energy, heat, vibration, and radiation cannot succeed because Source particles naturally return to Source and cannot be controlled by artificial means. Kim explains that many so-called aliens are hybrid Nazi experiments, while true non-Earth beings exist organically. She emphasizes that the Deep State, Alliance, and Axis powers are functionally the same groups competing for control of creation. Kim concludes that telekinesis, consciousness alignment, and direct connection to Source allow humans to bypass artificial systems, that the Machine cannot replicate true creation, and that humanity is entering a new period where Deep State influence collapses and Source-based creation is restored.
