Summary

Kim describes how military, intelligence, and financial operatives chased an AI-driven script tied to a failed Rapture timeline and a Yellowstone catastrophe scenario, while an insider she calls "the Cowboy" disclosed deep history: a 6thcentury BC offplanet origin, Andromeda-based cyborg control overlays, and preprogrammed 'days ending in Y.' She frames war as a chaos-harvesting mechanism rather than nation vs. nation, and ties the control architecture to the Debt System and a specific Debt Clock-depicted as a hovering tetrahedron in the Red Basin of Wyoming with cords to Homo sapiens and a Death Stone at its center. Multiple dates (Oct 1-2, 6; Full Moon) were pushed but failed; attempts to manifest a computer-generated Tree of Good and Evil and 'goo' entities into the Materium also failed, blocked by density shifts and loss of access. She asserts Chaos/Ego are programmatic forces reporting to Death, Etherium is harvested power, and dismantling the Debt System severs the energy routes underwriting control. She hints at a possible mutiny among human operatives and says they're prepared to "go it alone," declaring: the Debt Clock is gone and time is up for catastrophe-payments.
