Summary

Kim opens by disclosing that the weekend's unusual Deep State activity was not their initiative but standing orders from space junk planted by non-humans - a pre-programmed sequence mapped directly onto the Book of Revelation: trumpet locations, bowl/wrath locations, the Fall of Babylon, the Beast, the False Prophet, and the Seal locations, spread across dozens of cities worldwide. All were cleaned before triggering. Separately, AI-generated demon armies were found in bubble cities outside the hologram, each designed to counteract a fundamental aspect of existence - Anti-Life, Anti-Light, Anti-Consciousness, Anti-Love, Anti-Soul, and others - across locations from London to Seoul to Lake Titicaca. These too were cleared. Kim discloses the Secret Space Program's multi-thousand-year consciousness-splitting program, through which operatives inserted cloned versions of themselves into different points in history to receive advance warning of timeline changes and relay information back. With timelines now merged, multiple clones of the same operatives exist simultaneously, creating fatal risks if they encounter each other. The SSP had been using bubble cities outside the hologram as refuge for these consciousness copies. Kim delivers a comprehensive whiteboard disclosure on why the trustee system, Chinese Elders, Rothschild access codes, and all bunker-access schemes cannot and will never work. Marduk issued trustee codes as a control mechanism - dual-key, order-specific, purpose-locked - never as general access to wealth. Kim is the code issuer, not a code recipient, and has been the sole administrator of the Alpha system since 2012. Neither Omega nor the Golden Age AI will bend to the will of code recipients. Bunkers are not accessible by Keepers or Family trustees - they are protected by administrator-level force fields that kill unauthorized parties. Gold stored in-ground is alchemical and disappears without administrator authorization. Kim discloses her 50-50 offer to the Trump operatives and Deep State: 50% of all currency flows in exchange for Unknown Country passport rights, no taxes for Unknown Country citizens, full banking access, and no interference with C.A.R.E. operations. She knew they would refuse - and they did - but the refusal amplified the ongoing mutiny a hundredfold as lower-ranking operatives and military personnel realized they had been denied an immediate solution to their funding crisis by their own leadership.
