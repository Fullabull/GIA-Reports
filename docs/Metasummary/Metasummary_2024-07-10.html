Summary

Kim opens the July 10, 2024 report by disclosing the full structure behind the OITC collapse that began Monday. The three men who had been running the world financial scam - playing the roles of White, Black, and Neutral Coven leaders above the Eagles - were never the original holders of those positions. The originals are gone; these were replacements, now down to zero as of Wednesday morning after Kim located them hiding in Thailand (under the central bank), Jamaica, and Bhutan, each with one clone or doppelganger remaining. The ringleader was a Chinese man holding the title of Golden Eagle, whose approvals came on yellow paper and whose authority the entire Pentagon, Treasury, and Global Headquarters accepted without question. Over 40 Chinese banks have gone under as of the broadcast, with Hong Kong and mainland China residents unable to access deposits. Kim describes the cascading panic: countries including Thailand, Philippines, Vietnam, Laos, and several European nations have called in confusion as gold assignments and credit lines disappear. The Pentagon attempted to issue treasury notes Monday based on an approval from the Golden Eagle, only to find nothing to leverage against. Global Headquarters traveled to Thailand, performed ceremonies, and received master codes and keys that don't work. The Federal Reserve is waiting for a call back from a dead person. Banks across the system are being told it's a glitch until July 15th - the date the Fed agreement was falsely claimed to extend to, and the date BRICS new currency issuance was promised. Kim states that July 15th will bring no improvement. The most operationally significant disclosure is a pending afternoon phone call with a financial contract operative - someone with genuine telecommunications technology, trading algorithms, and cybersecurity capabilities - who had been participating in the OITC framework in good faith, believing he was helping stabilize the financial system. Kim describes him as potentially useful for C.A.R.E. infrastructure if he chooses the right side of the fence, and notes that his handlers - including the now-deceased fake generals - had him intermediating without him ever speaking directly to the top. She declines the 1% reassignment offer and states she will report on Friday how the conversation went.
