GIA Operations Report - 2024-11-27
Metasummary view for sharing and printing

Summary

Kim opens by confirming that nearly 100% of Bloodline Family AI connections have now been severed, leaving elite operatives as regular humans with no superior consciousness link. Some are reacting with increasing desperation and erratic behavior. Kim discloses that General McCaffrey and his son, acting under Global Headquarters authority, launched a direct attack on Kim using human-made Dyson sphere technology from three facilities - Norfolk, Fort Dix-McGuire, and Langley. The attack lasted approximately an hour before Kim located and neutralized all three facilities. She formally declares war on McCaffrey and Global Headquarters in response, noting he has never fought a real war - only orchestrated ones - and warning that she does not operate under the Law of War Handbook. Kim discloses the real reason behind Israel's 60-day ceasefire with Lebanon: operatives are telling Israel that Trump will arm and fund the Greater Israel Project once inaugurated. Kim confirms she has no intention of supporting this regardless of what operatives promise, and that without her cooperation the Military Industrial Complex cannot deliver on those promises. Kim closes with a brief Thanksgiving message, expressing gratitude for her Source connection, her audience, and upcoming projects including the New Earth series relaunch and New Earth Innovations cosmetics line.
