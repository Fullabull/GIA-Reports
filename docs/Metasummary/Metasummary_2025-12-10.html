Summary

Kim explains the origins, structure, and collapse of the Omniversal Matrix, an artificial system created when Lucerne separated from Source and attempted to replicate Creation without Breath. She describes how AI-generated matrix universes and worlds were overlaid onto Earth and humanity to distort perception, manipulate matter, consciousness, and memory, and redirect creative force away from Source. Central to the report is the distinction between an Aspect of Source and a Perspective of Source: humans possess a Sol, which cannot be sold, replicated, or separated because Breath belongs only to Source. Kim explains how artificial MIND systems rewrite memories and emotional responses to maintain control, but emphasizes that Sol-to-Sol communication through Love, Consciousness, and Breath remains intact. As Matrix systems fail and recovered Aspects return to Source, distortions are weakening, people are recognizing what is not real, and the restoration of Harmony and Rhythm marks the exit from the Matrix and the return to natural Creation.
