GIA Operations Report - 2024-09-18
Metasummary view for sharing and printing

Summary

Kim opens with a security disclosure: GIA intelligence intercepted Deep State conversations two nights prior in which leadership, aware of an incoming alignment, declared they would take humanity down with them rather than cede control. This was followed by a midnight planning session to conduct a snatch-and-grab operation against Kim in Durango - an attempt that failed before it could be executed. The main story is the exploding electronic devices across Lebanon, Syria, and Iraq. Kim traces the operation through semiconductor supply chains to Quest Global in Singapore, SMIC, and TSMC, with orders originating from Order of the Black Dragon and Dragon Family members in China and Japan. The mechanism was EMP-type frequencies sent through chip kernels via a backdoor system called GENISYS. The operation had full cooperation from the Pentagon and IDF, who participated in targeting discussions. Kim states it was a genocide test intended to scale globally across all electronic devices. The operation was shut down by mid-day, and the responsible parties are being hunted. Kim then covers the alignment that began overnight - an influx of Source atomic particles that dissipated remaining Dark and Neutral energy, cutting off Deep State access to the Omega System between 11 p.m. and 2 a.m. her time. She describes this as the atomic clock of Earth shifting to crystalline time, meaning only Source-origin atomic particles will remain. A side effect: GIA now controls the chip kernels SMIC and TSMC used for the attack, and plans to run healing frequencies through them. Kim closes with an update on oils restocking and signs off until Friday.
