Summary

This GIA Report details escalating conflicts between the Deep State, fallen Celestial aspects such as Wisdom, and Source-aligned forces during the ongoing 12/21 Portal. It describes the Deep State's attempt to secure victory by August 30 through the use of artificial portals, and outlines the origins of a cosmic war stemming from the division of Source into Light and Dark aspects. The narrative introduces the Ancients, Progenitors, and the false Celestial Wisdom, which created artificial realities and infected humanity with wormlets and parasites. Seven major public figures - including Putin, Trump, Xi, Elon Musk, King Charles, and others - are identified as hosts of Ancient aspects, sustained through death-and-rebirth covenants tied to the Absolute. These figures and their downlines feed energy into the Absolute via parasitic hive mind systems. Despite ceremonies and attempts (including one in Alaska) to anchor Wisdom directly, no human has been able to sustain it. As the covenant expires, Kim predicts the imminent collapse of the Dark Ancient structures, the death of parasites, and the restoration of Source's Reality. The report closes with warnings about false frequencies, reassurances of Source's promise, and anticipation of transitioning into the CARE plan and the Restoration.
