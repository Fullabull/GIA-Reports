Summary

Kim opens the August 7 report with Deep State operatives having spent the night in Durango surveilling her, trying to determine how she does what she does. She dismisses the effort and moves into current events. High-ranking members of Hamas and other Middle Eastern groups are quietly walking away after realizing their cause has been hijacked for political and financial ends that have nothing to do with their people's freedom. A Joint Chiefs of Staff member publicly stated that 'we must gain full control of that region' - Kim identifies 'we' as Global Headquarters and SSP interests, and their motive as a belief that controlling the Middle East will give them access to the Source Gate and its in-ground assets. Kim holds liens worth 2.1 quadrillion on those assets for the next 100 years, making any such plan financially meaningless. A $14 trillion contract originally floated by Nancy Pelosi to Blackwater for regional control is disclosed as still circulating inside the Pentagon. Iran's military threats against Israel are identified as entirely Chinese-directed - every order coming from Beijing, not from any imam or Islamic doctrine. China is simultaneously directing Israel, Iran, and the Russia-Ukraine conflict, driving all sides for its own purposes. Kim discloses the Money Magic System in detail: Deep State credit lines were not backed by real assets but by future credits - energy payments expected from Anti-Source during Lions Gate and other high-collection periods. These credits flowed through Omega into human financial systems as government credit lines, central bank allocations, and leveraged instruments. Overnight on August 6th, a foundational Source-Anti-Source covenant expired - one that had been in place for approximately 9,999 Earth years or 99 trips of the Milky Way around the Central Sun. With its expiration, the entire credit-generation mechanism collapsed. Golden Age AI does not recognize Omega's credit system, does not recognize future government payments tied to it, and does not recognize the Money Magic system at all. Margin calls went out across governments and central banks. Attempts to leverage customer bank balances via Omnibus accounts, SWIFT rail, and credit card float all failed. Kim confirms SWIFT is now under Golden Age control, not Rothschild. American Express is identified as a Department of Defense organization that monitors all cardholders globally. Hurricane Debbie is covered: the 'White Hats saving babies from tunnels' narrative is decoded - 'babies' in Deep State language means cocaine, and operatives on both sides are fighting over millions of dollars of cocaine that washed ashore on the Florida coast. Lions Gate is renamed Source Gate. Kim explains the mechanics: twice yearly (February and August), a very old connection between Source and Earth creates a super-wave of energy that Dark systems were designed to harvest via crystal hyperconductors, transmitters, and transmuter fields. The expiration of the covenant means this harvesting mechanism no longer has legal standing. A rumor is developing that U.S. Marshals and elements of Homeland Security want to work with the GIA to begin removing Trilateral Commission and NATO leadership from power; Kim notes it with cautious skepticism.
