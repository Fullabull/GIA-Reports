Summary

Kim opens by addressing criticism of her post-election report, clarifying that Trump is not a president to her but a member of the Order of the Black Sun with a specific cosmological role - and that the operatives running him represent a worldwide, not merely American, domination agenda. Kim discloses the full history and mechanics of the Conquer the Quest program - the millennia-long conflict between Light and Dark Forces across the Multiverse for control of creation. Earth is a keystone planet whose Light/Dark balance affects the entire Multiverse. With all alien overlords now gone, the Order of the Black Sun has declared itself the new leadership of the Dark side of Conquer the Quest, aspiring to become masters of the Universe. Kim notes the fundamental flaw: they would need to defeat Source itself. Trump is disclosed as one of seven humans ever born with a hybrid neutral/AI-generated soul, placed by Neutral Source specifically to reestablish balance at the threshold crossing from Dark Age to Golden Age. His soul's job was to bring darkness back when Light exceeded the 21% threshold. That role is now void - the Giver of Life Declaration filed in January 2023 moved the planet into Golden Age trajectory, the Balance Program was canceled, and the hybrid soul returned to Source in January 2022. What walks the planet as Trump consists of two clones - one from the Mongolia facility, one from Arkansas - plus several surgical look-alikes. Kim discloses the three main cloning facilities: the Ongi Monastery in Mongolia, under Arkansas, and the Bush Ranch in Paraguay. Kim discloses the Alpha Anti-Genesis Project - a Neutral Source regulator designed to wipe out approximately 48% of humanity and restore Dark/Light balance whenever the Light threshold is breached. It could only trigger on 11/11 at 11:11 a.m. The Deep State spent the weekend manning all 36 Neutral Source portals worldwide attempting to fire the Anti-Genesis Program. Key facilities were located at Mount Fuji/Tokyo Tower, Mitre Peak New Zealand, Platforma de Venus Mexico, Cradle Mountain Australia, Huayna Potosi Bolivia, Whitetop Mountain Virginia, and sites in the Andromeda Nebula and Aldebaran. Disease generation facilities were located beneath LIGO Laboratories at Caltech, Princeton, Washington State, and Louisiana. All facilities were hunted down and cleared. The Matterhorn was targeted for a soul swap attempt using Ivanka, which also failed. Nothing triggered at 11:11. Kim confirms King Charles is dead and an announcement is expected in December. Kim closes by noting all Neutral Source and Anti-Source portals are now clearing, psychic attacks should diminish, and the Golden Age AI should face no further interference once the portal cleanup completes by 11:00 p.m. Pacific on 11/11.
