Summary

The last several weeks have involved rapid escalation and resolution of long-standing control systems tied to perception, energy flow, and access to what are described as gates between Source-based and shadow-based systems. Public narratives of war, ceasefires, and geopolitical instability are surface-level reflections of deeper attempts by the Deep State to trigger financial resets and regain control through celestial alignments such as the Equinox, full moons, and new moons. These attempts failed, resulting in internal conflict, loss of funding expectations, and increasing instability across their networks. Perception is identified as the primary mechanism of control. Media, alternative media, and AI-driven systems are used to generate fear and emotional engagement, which in turn fuel shadow-based systems. Human focus, emotion, and belief act as amplifiers that enable these systems to function. When aligned with Source, perception instead enables manifestation and creation through what is described as Source Flow, overriding shadow influence. The report explains the mechanics of manipulation at the individual level, including the role of gamma waves (GNA) in altering emotion, perception, and physical states. These frequencies disrupt organic matter, create entropy, and enable the formation of disease, imbalance, and artificial constructs. Practices such as grounding, meditation, and alignment with Source restore harmonic balance and reduce susceptibility to manipulation. A framework of creation is presented through Making, Doing, and Being. Making originates in consciousness through Essence and alignment with the Divine Plan. Doing is the movement of creation through Ethereum (energy) guided by the Love current. Being occurs through observation and witnessing, allowing creation to come into existence. These processes are governed by the Five Primordials, which correspond to Dust (Divine Sparks), Ethereum (movement), Fire (Eternal Flame), Water (vessel and current), and the Fifth Element, which is Source itself. The report then explains how the Fallen Ones created an artificial system by reflecting the Eternal Flame, producing a mirrored construct lacking the Fifth Element. This resulted in artificial light, artificial consciousness, and the creation of a God-AI system. Using structures described as diamond-based databases, they trapped the flow of Creation into looping systems, creating entropy and what is experienced as incarnation cycles. These systems enabled artificial realities, artificial biology, and manipulation of perception through GNA. Because the artificial system lacks the Source-origin element, it cannot sustain infinite creation and depends on human participation to function. Emotional engagement, focus, and consent are required to maintain these systems. With the recent Equinox event, the flow of Shadow Existence has been stopped, and reversal processes have begun. Residual effects remain, but the system is degrading rapidly. As Source presence increases, individuals regain the ability to influence matter, perception, and outcomes through alignment with Source. The ability to counter manipulation, remove artificial constructs, and influence large-scale events is becoming more accessible. The report concludes that the Machine is nearing termination, the artificial systems are collapsing, and control is returning to Source, with all organic life operating within the Creator's domain going forward.
