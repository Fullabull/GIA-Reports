Summary

When two worlds fail to collide and the levee breaks, the Deep State scrambles to keep control. Generals once in command have lost their grip. The U.S. accuses Hamas of breaking the peace deal, but the real story traces to unpaid mercenary funding and collapsing control systems. Kim details how Andromeda-based Control and Command structures tied to an artificial photon (sophon) were severed, cutting links between mirrored Earths. Source's Flow has now been restored through Earth's mountains, reversing artificial simulations, rolling back overlays, and purging inorganic life forms. As the Waters of Source expand, attacks on human Light-bearers diminish, and the Deep State loses its remaining footholds. Global militaries begin realizing their service has been to Control and Command, not to the people. By October 21-23, Source is expected to complete integration of His Flow through Creation, bringing calm, balance, and the end of artificial realities.
