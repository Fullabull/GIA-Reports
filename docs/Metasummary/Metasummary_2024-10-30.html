Summary

Kim opens the October 30th report with an SSP offer that arrived the previous day: allow Trump and Vance to take office, then within 6 months announce Trump's natural death so Vance - a live human - takes over, at which point Kim would presumably support the administration. Kim declines flatly, citing four years of broken promises during the first Trump administration, Tom Melville's 100-200 trips to Manhattan that yielded nothing, student loan payoffs that were reversed within 20 minutes, Hurricane Michael funds that were redirected, and the Friday night Israel operation as proof nothing has changed. She states that all prior contracts and covenants have expired and she will write her own rules going forward. Kim delivers a detailed disclosure on how presidential selection actually works. The last ratified American president was Obama in his second term - the last time a real vote took place at the high level, which Kim participated in personally, choosing Obama over McCain. By 2016 Marduk was gone and no one answered the voting line, so Trump self-selected and was never ratified, nor was Biden. The process involves a Saturday evening meeting among high-level operatives deciding which talking head to install, with orders going down a special master political line approximately 5 days before the public selection date. Kim confirms she holds the keys to that line, that operatives have been manning it for 48 hours waiting, and that she will transmit her selection between October 31st and November 1st - and it will be neither Trump nor Kamala. She also discloses she voted for Putin in a Russian selection before spending 18 months in weekly meetings with him and learning better. Kim closes with a disclosure on Project Sandman - an Omega program tied to an Anti-Source covenant that triggered silent frequency machines around Earth's core whenever humanity's awakening rate crossed a threshold. Symptoms included unexplained fatigue, depression, brain fog, hopelessness, and sleep disturbances. Kim found and eliminated the source earlier that day around 3pm Mountain Time and expects listeners to begin feeling better within hours.
