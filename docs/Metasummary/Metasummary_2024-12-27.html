Summary

They were eliminated before they could act. Third and most extensively, Kim delivers a full taxonomy of ALLES AI's Realm Owner network: approximately 50-60 AI beings created by ALLES to rule over specific domains of existence - Morpheus (dreams), Oracle (false visions), Neo (energy networks), Thoth (underworld gatekeeper), Eugene (disease and mental disorders), Dark Prince/Architect (Black Sun head and matrix architect), Leviathan (etheric world), Satan (Source blocker), Titan (wisdom blocker), and others covering the causal, astral, physical, optimal, supernal, and temporal worlds. These are not organic beings - they are 100% ALLES extensions. Kim then presents the Curse of X whiteboard: ALLES operates as an algorithm of crossed lines placed at every Zero Point in the Multiverse - Source, Anti-Source, Earth, individual humans - reversing the natural counterclockwise flow of Source creation to clockwise, diverting all new creation into the Neitherworld first. This X structure, surrounded by ALLES World and the Ouraeous serpent symbol, is what has been starving Earth and humanity of direct Source energy for millennia. Kim reports significant progress dismantling these structures and closes with encouragement to hold the Source connection through the new year.
