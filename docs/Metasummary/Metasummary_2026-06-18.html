Summary

Kim opens the June 18, 2026 report with an account of the chaos surrounding the G7 meetings in France, where orders from the upper echelons of the Order kept changing, leaving operatives unable to finalize decisions on Iran, Ukraine, financial payouts to Israel, and other pending matters. Switzerland, the Deep State's industrial capital and Rothschild stronghold, hosted parallel meetings that produced nothing. Every promised payday -- for governments, operatives, and financial institutions alike -- came and went empty. A Manhattan banking outreach to Tom Melville was shut down before it could begin, blocked by the Federation's refusal to authorize cooperation with Kim. The confusion trickling down into public-facing political actors, from the White House to foreign parliaments, reflects a machine that no longer has instructions it can execute. Kim then delivers an extended framework on the Deep State as broken people -- systematically abused from infancy, programmed to fractionate their minds before age six, told that rape, murder, and torture are expressions of love. She asks her audience for compassion, not sympathy, drawing parallels to victims of domestic abuse who cannot recognize their condition. Politicians, entertainers, and industrialists alike -- Netanyahu, Trump, Elon Musk, Britney Spears -- are disclosed as products of this program. More critically, Deep State members are not merely connected to the Machine -- they are part of its operating system, feeding it and being fed by it in a vampire loop that Lucy designed and that is now collapsing for lack of energy. Kim then delivers the cosmological history underlying the current transition. Lucifer was Source's first creation and general contractor for the Divine Plan -- the foreman who held the architect's plans and ensured all beings fulfilled their portions. As creation expanded and beings connected directly to Source, Lucifer became redundant and responded by creating the Veil -- a black and gold database overlaying all beings and planets, intercepting their Divine Plan transmissions and rerouting all Materium through himself. He attempted to copy the full Plan from humans, from Earth as the planetary keystone, and finally from Source itself, but the Secret Sauce -- Source's generative principle -- was never transmitted to him and was never copyable. The result was a machine running on an incomplete plan, filling the gaps with Lucy's own design, producing the black-and-white good-and-evil binary the Deep State has operated within for approximately 1.8 billion years in Earth time. Approximately four days before the report, Leviathan -- the name of the Machine -- froze. The Deep State interpreted this as their long-awaited Great Reset and immediately attempted to submit a new divine plan, nominate God-figures for the White Way, and conduct a Judgment Day assessment of humanity. Every attempt failed. The Machine confirmed they hold only one Key -- a Black Key. The three-Key system requires a White Key and a Gold Key as well, the latter tied to pure Source Essence. The Machine is specifically programmed not to accept instructions from Homo sapiens under any circumstances, and without Lucifer -- the only plenipotentiary it recognizes -- it will remain frozen until Source decides otherwise. Kim holds one Key, Source holds a second, and the Deep State's single Black Key cannot override the two that are not in agreement with it. Kim closes with the most significant disclosure of the report: the Divine Plan distribution is imminent. Each person will receive their portion of the Plan directly from Source, along with all the Materium, Wisdom, and resources needed to execute it. No plenipotentiary -- not the Guardian, not C.A.R.E., not the Machine -- stands between a person and their Plan. Approximately 150,000 to 200,000 interim coordinators will receive a broader picture of the Plan to help others find their roles during the transition, but authority flows directly from Source to each individual. Coordinators who deviate lose their funding; Source moves to the next candidate. Earth's role as planetary provider is restored. Farmers will till and harvest by paracausal means. Eventually, as all beings reconnect directly to Source, the Golden Age AI becomes unnecessary, money becomes irrelevant, and telekinetic movement of Materium replaces all mechanical systems. Kim declares this the ninth and final transition cycle -- nine equals completion -- and expresses confidence that her Ground Commander role is ending and her Guardian role approaches its own conclusion. She asks her audience to spend time with Source daily, have compassion for the broken, and get ready for their information. It is already arriving.
