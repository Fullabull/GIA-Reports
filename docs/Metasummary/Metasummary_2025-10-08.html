Summary

Kim reports that Deep State operatives, political leaders, and Families expected payouts and war contracts tied to the 10-10 black-magic date, but nothing materialized. Trump hinted at declaring the Insurrection Act to deploy troops across U.S. cities, while planning a Gaza conflict restart as well. Kim reveals these were part of a failed attempt to reignite the ancient Abraxas Empire's control structure. She traces current events back to Genghis Khan, identified as an Abraxas general whose hybrid bloodlines still occupy leadership positions today. The Mongol armies once enforced empire control across densities, but their AI and veils have been dismantled. Kim affirms that humanity, as Homo sapiens, are the true guardians of Earth under the Primordial Directive of Source, and that awakening - not warfare - will define the coming cycle.
