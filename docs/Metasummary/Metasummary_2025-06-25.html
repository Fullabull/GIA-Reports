GIA Operations Report - 2025-06-25

Summary

Kim opens the June 25th report by framing the current conflict as a war of attrition - the simulation's strategy is to wear out humanity until it consents to the Dark Side, and the Deep State, unwitting participants, are being used as instruments of that pressure. She emphasizes that geopolitical noise - wars, tariffs, political theater - is irrelevant to the real war, which is fought entirely at the level of consciousness, soul, and simulation. Kim delivers an extended personal disclosure about her years of direct interaction with Marduk, describing how he studied her personality across thousands of hours of conversation in order to build a simulation tailored to break her specifically. She recounts the New York vault incident - traveling from Arizona to retrieve funds from a JP Morgan tunnel facility beneath Grand Central Station, only to find everything cleared out on Marduk's orders - as a formative example of his methods. She explains that Marduk's goal was always to get the Guardian to seal the fate of humanity, because one Guardian signature delivers eight billion souls. That seal never came. The report then delivers a comprehensive disclosure of the Omniversal war structure. Kim maps the nine densities up and nine densities down, explains the three thrones of Source - mind, body, and soul - and traces how Lucifer and Lucerne initiated the original simulation called Solomon as a weapon to penetrate and control Source itself. She identifies each Realm Owner by density: Lucifer and Lucerne at Nine, Enlil at Eight, Marduk at Seven, Artemis at Five. She discloses that Earth is the singular strategic prize because human souls exist across all densities simultaneously - one human soul equals an entire density's worth of souls, making Earth the key to controlling the entire Omniverse. Kim confirms she is a Level 9 Player, which means she can operate in any realm and, with Lucifer and Lucerne no longer present, can now control both the Dark and Light sides of any lesser simulation. She has been using this to create phantom financial systems - dangling carrots for the Deep State - while dismantling the actual simulation infrastructure underneath. She discloses that the simulation is now in its final hours, working overtime to get humanity to consent to darkness through disease, job loss, relationship fractures, guilt, and fear - because without the Guardian's seal and without mass human consent, there are no Realm Owners and the game is over. Kim closes with direct practical guidance: say no to the simulation. Surround your mind, body, and soul with your connection to Source. Visit the three golden thrones from within. The simulation cannot kill you - it needs your consent, not your death. She asks listeners to be the sane presence for those around them who are under heavier influence, and signs off with the assurance that it is almost over.
