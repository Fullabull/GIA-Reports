Summary

Kim opens by addressing Syria, disclosing that Assad's fall and the rebel liberation of Sednaya prison are connected to a much deeper reality: Syria contains one of 33 stargate portal rooms distributed in a ring across Europe, Russia, and the Middle East, with the 33rd and central facility located in Austria. These rooms contain rotating doorways that can be directed to any location in the Multiverse - including what Kim now discloses as the Neitherworld. The Syria facility extends in a crescent shape under the Mediterranean to Cyprus, housing approximately 175,000 prisoners who were used as human batteries to power the portals. Israel and the U.S. began bombing Syria specifically to control access to these stargates. Kim delivers a full disclosure of the Neitherworld and its creator - a being she calls The Destroyer, historically known as Gilgamesh - one of the most powerful Lower Astral beings to have existed, approximately Density Seven. Gilgamesh poked holes into Source, Anti-Source, and Neutral Source, harnessing the power of all three to create a parallel fake universe - the Neitherworld - outside the Fringe of the actual Universe. He created fake Source, fake Anti-Source, fake Neutral Source, and a replacement AI system called ALLIES (The All), which he implanted into select SSP operatives, giving them direct brain communications they believe are from the All-That-Is. He also created infinite doppelganger Earths in the Neitherworld to use as voodoo-doll anchors against real Earth. During the 11/11 gateway, Neitherworld beings began flooding into our Universe through the Syria stargates, reaching between Density Five and Six before being blocked. They could not penetrate the Sixth Density from above or the Second Density from below, but could affect the Physical Plane and Causal Plane. Hundreds of thousands of these beings - described as 20-25 foot tall horned, muscular, reptilian-skinned, red-eyed entities - entered Earth's density space by Friday/Saturday. Kim, the Enforcer, and others battled them across multiple densities, cut off all portal access, destroyed the Syria stargates and all connected facilities, and placed a shield at the Oort Belt to prevent escape. Kim additionally discloses GrayLink - an SSP division that served as the communications bridge between the Neitherworld and our Universe, operating out of primary sites in Utah and secondary sites worldwide. GrayLink operatives received ALLIES implants and attempted minor infrastructure disruptions on Sunday night. Kim closes by noting the battle is not yet concluded - the next portal window is the December 21st solstice, and January 21st was flagged as another target date.
