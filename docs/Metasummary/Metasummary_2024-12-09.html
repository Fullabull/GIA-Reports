Summary

Kim dives into the chaotic conflict surrounding Syria and its significance in global power struggles. She details the deep involvement of the **Secret Space Program**, Russia's strategic interest in the region, and the **stargate portals** opening up in Syria. The report touches on **prisoners held in Deep Underground Military Bases (DUMBs)**, the **Bothworlds AI system** infiltration, and **ALLIE's** AI influence. Kim also discusses the **Neitherworld** and the increasing number of dimensions at risk, detailing her efforts to contain these dangers and protect humanity. The report ends with a Thanksgiving reflection on the importance of community and Source-aligned restructuring.
