Summary

Kim opens by addressing the rough weekend humanity experienced, disclosing that the Secret Space Program used keys provided by Michael, Gabriel, and Raphael during their permitted 24-hour cleanup window to access Celestial Corridors - quantum tunnel systems spanning all densities of the Alphaverse and Omegaverse - and attempted to power them up using human creative and destructive energy as fuel. Kim delivers a comprehensive whiteboard disclosure on Celestial Corridors: vast multi-density quantum tunnels structured like hotel vestibules with 1,296,000 doors per level, each door opening to a different star system, time, or being. Corridors are powered by a one-for-one exchange of human creative or destructive energy - every act of creation or destruction generates credits that allow corridor operators to create or destroy within the corridor's accessible densities. The Berlin corridor reached up to Density Seven. The SSP, believing they were accessing financial control systems, were actually just generating space junk between densities and creating AI-generated species in the Lower Astral. They broke through to between Density Five and Six but could not enter actual planes of existence, only the spaces in between. On Saturday, they created cyborg-reptilian beings in the Lower Astral who attempted to enter Earth - and failed, because Kim and Source are the Gatekeepers. Kim discloses how Black Magic operates through corridors: Covens and Black Magicians were granted limited keys to certain corridor rooms by the Celestials who ran Densities 1-3, allowing them to weave magic against specific targets whose signatures existed in AI systems - through photos, IDs, bank records. The current corridor attacks caused physical and emotional symptoms in humanity including back pain, headaches, cold symptoms, chest tightness, and random illness - all generated by the SSP's attempts to power the corridor, which drew energy from every human on Earth simultaneously. Kim discloses the Assemblage Point - the first spark of physical creation in each human being, the point at which the first cell formed and split. It exists in the chest region for most adults, is movable, and connects the human to both Source and the Dark Side through a heartbeat-like dark/light alternation. It was the primary attachment point for corridor-based attacks on individuals. Kim confirms all corridors have now been eliminated. She closes by noting that direct Source connection - without regulators or intermediaries - is the ultimate goal of Earth's restoration, and that once these systems are gone, humans will be able to co-create directly with Source and with beings from other densities.
