GIA Operations Report - 2024-09-25
Metasummary view for sharing and printing

Summary

Kim opens with a detailed announcement of UNN's restructured programming format: the World Situation Report transitions to the GIA Operations Report, running Monday/Wednesday/Friday alongside a revamped regular news segment from Sunny featuring longer in-depth stories, field messenger participation, and shareable content designed for audiences just waking up. A separate New Earth series covering alternative health, technology, and vendors will run Tuesday/Thursday/Saturday. Kim frames the rename explicitly - the Global Intelligence Agency branding allows a more credible entry point for newcomers than the World Situation Report format. Kim then addresses widespread impersonation of GIA personnel at the UN General Assembly, confirming that Tom Melville is the only authorized public-facing GIA representative besides Kim herself. She details how GIA agent identity verification works, how funds are deployed within 20 minutes of commitment, and why every attempt to route Kim's resources under the skirt of Langley 5, the Japanese Black Dragon, or other Order factions will fail. Heads of state and operatives at the General Assembly are now calling for help after promised funds from fake GIA representatives failed to materialize. The operational centerpiece is the arrival of the Golden Blanket - 100% unencumbered Source Lifeforce beginning to saturate Earth, starting at the Iran-Iraq gate the night before the report. Kim connects this to a vision from 2014/2015 in which she saw herself in the desert near that same location, slamming a rod into the ground, seven coins spreading gold to the seven continents. By broadcast time the blanket is approximately 80% complete, spreading elliptically outward from the Iran-Iraq origin through China, across the poles, and closing in on the Americas. Kim describes this as the beginning of full re-terraforming - Source taking the reins directly. Real-world signs are already appearing: lilac bushes blooming out of season in the northern US, the Sahara visibly greening from satellite. Deep State figures attempted to bribe Kim to stop it; others called on old lines trying to reach beings who no longer exist.
