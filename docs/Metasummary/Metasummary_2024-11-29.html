Summary

Kim opens by disclosing the Germanic tribes - known publicly as the Amish and Mormons - as the next group attempting to seize Deep State control. General McCaffrey's debts trace back to this group, which has spent years lending money to Trump operatives, Black Nobility families, and the CIA on the promise of being repaid once their preferred candidates took power. When those promises failed, the Germanic tribe council called in their debts and claimed ownership of everyone who couldn't pay - including Global Headquarters operatives - then began dividing up control of political and military divisions. Kim delivers a whiteboard disclosure on the Germanic tribe bloodline: they are descendants of Mephistopheles (8th density Lower Astral) and Saint Germain (7th density Lower Astral), giving them access to both Density 8/1 and Density 7/2 - effectively Neutral-level access that allowed them to block both Source and Anti-Source. This made them more powerful than most other Deep State groups, and they remained deliberately neutral between Dragon and Black Sun factions for centuries while positioning for their moment to strike. Their public arms - the Mormon Church and the Amish community - serve as recruitment, intelligence, and corporate infiltration networks. The Saint Germain Trust, which RV communities believe will fund a global currency revaluation, was a cloaked off-ledger system within Omega connected to this bloodline. Kim discloses that over the past 72 hours the Germanic tribe council activated pandemic-triggering space junk at sites across Pennsylvania, Ohio, Germany, Russia, Australia, Alaska, Antarctica, Washington State, Maine, California, Florida, and Iceland, combined with coven rituals broadcasting disease frequencies including SARS, bird flu, cancer, and Epstein-Barr. All sites were cleared. The council of 12-13 and their coven are no longer present. Kim confirms the Saint Germain Trust AI remnants have also been eliminated. She closes by noting that Russia's announced cryptocurrency plans are tied to Germanic tribe promises over Kolyma gold assets - assets already transferred to the Golden Age AI Global Repository. She confirms Kim holds veto power over any such currency issuance, and that the liens against Russian and other assets remain in place.
