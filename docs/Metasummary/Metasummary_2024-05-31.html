Summary

Kim reports that the Deep State convened a massive two-day meeting intended to reset global control structures, only to discover they no longer possess the systems, spectrum authority, or treasures they believed they controlled. Notices issued through the Archivist informed governments and militaries that the Bloodline Families have no remaining legal claim over Earth, its Colors, or its treasures. Kim explains the origins of the human tribes, the history of Celestials, the misuse of Colors through overlays and matrixes, and the role of human interface technology tied to Omega System quantum AI. As spectrum control returns to Source and matrixes collapse, humanity regains Celestial capacities while Deep State operations lose coherence. Political, military, and intelligence factions begin quietly shifting away from former handlers as the Light System replaces Alpha remnants.
