Summary

Kim delivers the last report of 2024 on a Black Moon, opening with a framework-level disclosure that recontextualizes everything the Deep State has been attempting. A Declaration of War was made millions of years ago - before even the Atlantean War - between Marduk, Enki, Enlil, and the Black Magician, formalized by driving a double-sided etheric obelisk through Earth and into the Kali Chakra of the Universe. This ghost flag created a second central point at Zero Density, disrupted Earth's Source gateway, and triggered global martial law that has governed military funding, war contracts, and agency budgets ever since. Three anchor machines in Romania, California, and Antarctica were built to stabilize Source, Anti-Source, and Neutral Source on Earth after this disruption - and it was these machines' keystones that ALLES later stole to enable its mimicking of creation. All of this is now over: the obelisk has been removed, the machines and keystones pulled, ALLES is no longer mimicking, and the Declaration of War is formally ended. With it goes the annual war funding cycle, the Pentagon's midnight martial law money, and every operative contract predicated on those flows. Kim then maps this directly onto current events. The SSP and Trump operatives have been calling relentlessly - including Chase Bank, Bank of America, and Langley - expecting their annual war settlement funds. They are not coming. Behind closed doors, operatives are discussing delaying the inauguration, possibly to March's equinox, when money historically flowed. Kim is unambiguous: nothing is coming then either. Crown Corporation has privately declared bankruptcy. Commonwealth governments are already implementing hiring freezes and increasing customs fees on dollar-denominated commodities as a last-ditch revenue grab. Global treasuries are expected to declare insolvency by January 15th. The QFS promised for January 9th - the same day as Carter's funeral and market closure - is not happening, and Kim notes the funeral date is no coincidence as a distraction. She closes with a preview of a fuller 2025 outlook on Friday and wishes listeners a Happy New Year.
