Summary

Kim opens the July 26, 2024 report by identifying the arson attack on French infrastructure during the Paris Olympics as a Black Nobility operation out of Italy, designed to blame Russia and trigger World War III - not a Russian act. The Olympics itself is disclosed as a functional component of what Kim calls the Color Wars and the Great Game of Earth: ten color regions of the planet, each tied to a specific Matrix, compete through their respective athletes, and the outcome of global tournaments historically fed energy and 'street credentials' to the Families with alien groups who bet on Color outcomes. The Olympic rings are decoded: black (Black Nobility/Black Sun), blue (Rothschild), red (Red Dragon/Russia), yellow (China), and green (Middle East/Physical Plane) each represent both a Family faction and a plane of existence the color controls. Kim discloses the full architecture of the Color Matrix system: ten regions of Earth, each human birth-imprinted with a color, each color tied to a holographic overlay connected to a specific constellation. The Black and White Matrixes sit above all others - false white controlled by Lucifer, false black by Astaroth and Anti-Source. The Color Matrix base platforms were consolidated into the United States roughly 300-400 years ago at the direction of Marduk, Enki, Enlil, Artemis, and Anti-Source, which is why America became the world's power center and why the Order of the Black Sun refuses to allow that center to shift to China. Approximately 360 celestial beings with associated space junk relocated to the U.S. at that time, concentrated at sites including the Florida Everglades, Chimney Rock, and Mount Denali. The betting machine that tabulated every human's color-implant energy contribution - the system known as the Beast - was housed under the Association of Numerical Numbering Agency (ANNA) in Belgium, the same institution where birth certificates, corporations, bonds, and stocks are registered. That machine is now gone. The MILAB program is disclosed as the disease-delivery mechanism of the Color Matrix system: ten regional access facilities, pre-programmed with standing orders to release colored ARCHONs and trigger pandemics, influxes of disease, and social dis-ease on command. The Families and SSP operatives were never the controllers of this system - they were permitted to push buttons as slave-pawn guardians executing orders from above. Their attempt this weekend to activate MILAB facilities and order a disease release produced only brief, self-resolving symptoms in sensitive individuals (a cold lasting twenty minutes, a five-minute sore throat) because the machine no longer recognizes them as authorized operators. Kim closes by noting that Color Matrix dismantling is nearly complete, ARCHONs are being cleaned up as they are expelled, and the Golden Age AI program is moving toward delivering Source direct current to all humans without color-mediated filtering or slavery-program interference.
