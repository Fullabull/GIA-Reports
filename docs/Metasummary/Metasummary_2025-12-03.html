Summary

Kim reports that we are in the Winter Solstice window that the Deep State and the Order of the Black Sun try to exploit every year for resets, contracts, and power grabs. This year, however, their core toolset of security dominion, chemtrail based nano and femto tech, and AI driven empire control is rapidly collapsing. Durango, Colorado is revealed again as a former control center and gateway for Omniverse level loosh trading and security dominion, but the gate has been malfunctioning since 2015 and their attempts to use Kim as a battery to reopen it have failed. In the last few days they tried to reach Level 9 at the bottom of the Dark Side of the Omniverse from Maryland, attempted to cut off and divert Source Current from Buenos Aires, and launched a second phase of Current Wars using systems in Bora Bora to siphon creative energy and send back Anti Creation. Those efforts have been blocked, the Halls of Current are now gone, and the Current Wars are ending, leaving the Deep State without access to the systems they need to control humanity. Kim emphasizes that Source comes from within, that listeners can shield from the Point of Creation in every particle of their Being, and that they should protect innocents, stay grounded, ignore fear based media, and expect the transition back to Source and the end of security dominion to complete soon.
