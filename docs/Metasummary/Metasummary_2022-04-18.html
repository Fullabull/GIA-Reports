Summary

Kim explains that the old Multiverse control scaffold (Metatron's Cube) is dissolving and is being replaced with a Source-aligned Tesseract structure. A strengthened Gegenschein alignment and periodic pulses from the Central Sun are re-establishing a direct Source connection while cutting off Lower-Astral access paths (e.g., Saturn-Moon Matrix portals). She says this is triggering backlash: tech interference (e.g., Summit at Oak Ridge), attempted Dark-side incursions, and heavy chemtrail activity intended to energize nanotech and ARCHON links. She reports that the Abraxas Empire fell on April 3 and ties black-market/finance structures (Jay's Treaty, bond/derivative scaffolds on birth certificates, IRS coupon cycles, MERS re-leveraging) to a system now unwinding. Looking ahead to April 24 (and, in notes, April 26 for 'balance/order/completion'), she anticipates increasing pulses every 3-4 days, human senses and cognition amplifying, and parallel liberation work on other planets.
