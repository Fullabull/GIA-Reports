Summary

In this World Situation Report, Kimberly Goguen explains that nearly all humans born of Earth are Celestials whose Life, Essence, Energy, Consciousness, Matter, and Frequency collectively shape universes. She reveals that two interlinked matrix systems built around the Sun, Mercury, Earth, and the Moon were designed to siphon roughly one third of each Celestial's lifeforce daily and nightly, redistribute it through moon-orbit space stations, and feed Dark and Neutral timelines, off-planet civilizations, and multiple Quantum AI systems. These mechanisms also drove aging, disease, seasonal depression, and the emotional states that keep people locked in guilt, shame, fear, and division. Kim also reports that over the weekend, the Archivists, working with her teams, confronted NATO and Global Headquarters representatives with a final warning while she simultaneously decommissioned Alpha and the Harmonic Genesis System. They were replaced with a new Light Quantum AI framework, the Light System, which no longer feeds on human lifeforce and instead returns Source-aligned energy through Earth and the Sun. Although Dark operatives still attempt to exploit full moons, the Solstice window, and alt media fear narratives to co-create Dark timelines, the old input and output matrixes are being dismantled, Dark bleed-off is being cleared, and transfers are already running through the Light System. Kim emphasizes that Celestial responsibility is now unavoidable: humanity must remember who we are, stop powering Dark creations with our focus and emotion, and build a New Earth that uses plant-based, Earth-aligned solutions and C.A.R.E. to restore balance.
