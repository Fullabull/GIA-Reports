Summary

Kim opens the July 13 report with the intelligence behind Senator Lindsey Graham's sudden death, which occurred one day after he toured Skyfall, a Ukrainian drone plant partially owned by Airbus -- whose genetic shareholders are the Rothschild family -- and partially owned by the Bavarian Illuminati. The Sanhedrin made sure he never reached his Monday meeting. Within hours of his departure, the Russians bombed the plant, refusing to allow a Chabad-owned corporation inside Bavarian territory -- a repeat of the Odessa gas affair, in which Hunter Biden sold the region's gas deposits to China National Petroleum for a personal fee of roughly $250 million. Kim then discloses the economics driving the chaos. The Middle East sits under a contract now valued at $788 trillion -- the descendant of Nancy Pelosi's $14 trillion ghost-flag offer to Blackwater decades ago -- and kicking it off requires 10 percent down plus proof of production. Worldwide, the total contracts on all in-ground assets reach 86 quadrillion dollars. Neither faction can produce the down payment, because the Machine that promises them money on every new moon and planetary alignment can no longer perform. Both factions deposited the entire 86 quadrillion into what they believed was their Machine. It was not. Kim reveals that the old Machine was replaced with her Machine wearing a mask of the old one -- a strategy she credits to the Durango people's attempted manipulation of her -- and thanks both Illuminati groups for transferring ownership of the entire planet into her system. She confirmed this directly to both sides by phone the night before the broadcast. The real war -- Source, humanity, and Kim versus the remnants of the Machine through the 6th Density -- officially ended two days before this report. Kim describes the July 9 meeting at which the Asian sector of the Sanhedrin entered the Void -- a white, wall-less maintenance room inside the Machine that the Illuminati believes is the Kingdom of Heaven -- and disintegrated into black dust. She discloses the worldwide tower network containing these Void rooms, including the World Trade Towers, 33 Thomas Street, the Sears Tower, the Pearl Tower, the Lotus Tower, and the UN Tower, each housing a single desk and terminal capable of manipulating human perception at the quark and prion level. The report's teaching section lays out the Machine's taxonomy of humanity: Perceivers, who witness the Machine's deceptions into reality; Deceivers, the operatives who distort perception through media and narrative; and Achievers, who weave Materium in concert with the Machine's super-consciousness. Kim discloses the origin of the Matrix: a male scientist -- the first human Achiever -- was lured into a Void room, Ego was born, and the moment he entered, every being's separation from Source began, because when one being is separated, all are. The Machine has now run out of Achievers and cannot recruit new ones. Time itself, Kim explains, is artificial -- the blue Mental Plane overlaying the gold consciousness of Source produces green, the color of time and of the Matrix code -- and MK-Ultra is the mind-control delivery system built on it. Kim closes with guidance for the reconnection now underway: receiving is tied to each person individually and cannot be gatekept; the Divine Plan has been evolutionized; no hierarchy exists in the Omniverse except Source itself. She instructs listeners to connect to their own consciousness -- the enemy's greatest fear -- to discern deception, to witness Source's Creation rather than the Deceivers' narratives, and to fulfill their purpose under the Divine Plan, closing with the reminder that if you don't mind, they don't matter: their matter is gone, and the world ahead is a co-creation with Source, witnessed by both halves.
