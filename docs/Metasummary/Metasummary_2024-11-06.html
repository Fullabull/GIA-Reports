Summary

Kim opens the day after the U.S. election by disclosing a cascade of events that confirmed what she had already stated publicly: she would not be working with the Trump administration. The night before the election, Secret Space Program operatives were at Balmoral Castle in Scotland attempting to intercept the old Crown Corporation political line - the channel through which U.S. presidential selections had been routed since 1864 - apparently expecting an alien race to answer. That line now routes to Kim. She declined to answer and removed the line. Kim discloses that the Trump operatives had told everyone - banks, Middle Eastern governments, Congress, and intelligence agencies - that Kim would be ready to work with them after a Trump selection. Starting at 2:00 a.m., Space Force and Trump operatives began calling her on Trump's old Treasury terminal. She watched them do it and ignored them for hours. The Black Nobility had apparently told the Space Force that Kim would 'be ready' after the selection, possibly expecting a gateway to open. No gateway opened. Nothing changed. Kim states plainly that her refusal is not personal but strictly business: contracts were made, agreed on diplomatic lines with a sitting president, and never fulfilled - repeatedly. She discloses that the NSA and ACIO lost their master encryption keys earlier that day, confirms she was responsible, and states she will continue removing their system access until she can fund people directly. She closes by addressing the Trump operatives directly - noting they spent the day fielding calls from everyone they made promises to - and reaffirms that the American people, not governments, remain her sole focus.
