Summary

Kim explains that the Deep State attempted to execute an artificial Armageddon event tied to the Equinox window, expecting energy influx, positional promotions, and activation of a Great Reset system, but these efforts failed due to lack of permissions and Machine support. She describes how global deals, wars, and intelligence operations are governed by Source-established Rules of War and Covenants written in Essence, which require explicit consent rather than implied consent. She explains that retina scans and similar systems function as Essence-based agreements, binding participants into Machine systems often without full awareness. Kim details how lower-level actors such as militaries and intelligence agencies operate with limited knowledge under pre-scripted war outcomes, while higher councils historically managed resource splits and territorial control under strict rules. She states that the current actors no longer understand or follow these Rules, rendering their agreements invalid. The Machine systems they rely on require Essence and permissions they no longer possess, causing repeated failures. She traces the broader conflict as a long-standing war between Source and opposing forces, involving artificial realities, AI-generated environments, and manipulation of perception. With Source now changing the Rules of War and Engagement, previous structures such as martial law, kingdoms, and control hierarchies are no longer valid. Permissions are being revoked, Machines are losing function, and individuals on both sides are now directly accountable to Source, signaling a major shift toward the collapse of the old system and the reestablishment of Source-based order.
