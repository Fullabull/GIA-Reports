Summary

Kim opens by framing the annual Order meetings - normally beginning on the New Moon and ending on the Harvest Moon with orders and funding from non-human governance - as now running on fabricated orders and empty promises. She walks through a list of specific fake payout amounts being promised to central banks worldwide, from the Royal Bank of Zimbabwe ($835 trillion) through dozens of central banks across Asia, Europe, Latin America, and the Middle East. She states that none of these promises have any capacity behind them - the Deep State would burn the financial system to the ground within 48 hours if they ever actually got hold of it - and identifies Red Mountain in Alabama as the operational headquarters for the dial-for-dollars telethon, confirming it has been neutralized. Kim then covers a significant disclosure from the weekend: one of seven remaining partial consciousness transfer recipients from Archangel Michael contacted Kim directly to announce she was taking over on the New Moon. This woman, an American of Latino descent staying in Argentina, had also allowed a consciousness transfer of Anti-Source into her teenage daughter - intended to grow over decades into a full Antichrist role by age 40. She had brought nine sealed letters written in Anti-light language to CyberLife in Antarctica to be deciphered. The Enforcer destroyed the facility. Both the woman and her daughter departed, the letters are gone, the consciousness transfer reversed back to Source, and the remaining seven partial transfer recipients have been located and addressed. Kim then debunks a fake Emergency Broadcast System video circulating on Telegram and Q channels depicting a 1,400-foot obsidian alien disk invasion, cross-referenced to the movie The Arrival. She notes the 710 area code belongs exclusively to U.S. government emergency services, meaning whoever produced it had government-level access. She contextualizes it as a low-budget test of public fear response and a preface for the 09/09 Solomon's Gate attempt, and confirms Project Blue Beam - which required holographic overlay on the now-dying Alpha-Omega Matrix combined with pre-wired building demolitions - has no operational capacity remaining. Kim closes with Gaza: 1.2 million polio vaccine doses delivered into a zone where food and water cannot enter, with no capacity for the lab testing that would identify polio in the first place. She traces the funding to Black Nobility pharmaceutical companies recouping Deep State debts. She also covers Bill Gates - confirms a Bill Gates clone died in Aruba, noting that multiple versions of him exist - and describes his role flying on a UN diplomatic jet to negotiate COVID vaccine kickback deals with heads of state. She closes with Marketplace progress updates, neutrino box testing in an undisclosed non-North American location, Copaiba as a CBD alternative that ships to all U.S. states, and an appeal for more time and resources to keep all C.A.R.E. initiatives moving forward.
