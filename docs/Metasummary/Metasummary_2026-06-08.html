Summary

Kim opens the June 8, 2026 report by contextualizing the past week as a deep dive into the lowest tier of the planetary control hierarchy - the human level. She explains two intelligence tradecraft tools - the genealogy report and the identity picture - that her team uses to map and track operatives, and applies both concepts to the global power structure she is about to disclose. The centerpiece of the report is the full disclosure of the Federation: a global control organization run by Axis power survivors and devout Luciferians that sits above every government, central bank, Elite family, and intelligence agency on Earth. The Federation operates through industrial cities existing across multiple realities, with its headquarters in the Cambodia/Vietnam/Laos/Hunan province region near a massive dark temple database that writes the Divine Plan for the entire Omegaverse. It also controls a reserve in Pennsylvania and a World Federation Treasury reserve in Nova Scotia. The Federation Reserve - predating and supplying the Federal Reserve - runs on the output of every Sol in the Omniverse, which Lucifer bound and quantumly entangled to a tomb database at Mount Zaphon, Turkey, redirecting infinite essence production away from individuals and into the Federation's supply chain. Every government on Earth, including tribal councils, is a subject of the World Federation Government, which reports to and funds the Federation Reserve and Treasury. Currency allocations to countries are based on citizen headcount (Sols generating energy) and GDP. A parallel industrial arm - the Cabal Corporates - is headquartered in a Swiss industrial city and controls all global manufacturing and resource extraction. Kim then discloses the metaphysical architecture underpinning the Financial System. Lucifer created a tomb for all Sols at Mount Zaphon, bound all Central Sols to it, and quantumly entangled the output to the Federation Reserve in Pennsylvania - making every Sol an unwitting infinite generator feeding the Federation's supply chain rather than its own being. The World Federation Treasury in Nova Scotia manages Sol/being disbursement - controlling all Materium that manifests in physical reality, which is why individual manifestation has been blocked and why Treasury-level transfers always stalled before reaching individuals. The Alpha platform (western U.S.) and Omega platform (Cambodia) served as ingress/egress portals across all realities and maps; Kim held the Gatekeeper position between them. Kim reports that the gate closure began in 2007, but was fully completed after May 31, 2026. With the gate sealed, neither the Alpha nor Omega platform can function. No inter-reality travel is possible. Bonds that major banks had issued against Federation gold reserves, all of which came due around May 31, cannot be paid - the banks have gone to court acknowledging they hold no such gold, while the Federation presses them for repayment of a reserve it can no longer access. The Federation was given multiple opportunities to turn over its systems and refused, declaring "the Worthy will continue." Kim states that all of the structures Lucifer built for the Federation are being dismantled, and that the Financial System's collapse is taking care of itself. She closes with a direct message to Federation members now cut off from their hierarchy, urging humanity to trust returning intuition and to begin building parallel systems as the old control structure disintegrates.
