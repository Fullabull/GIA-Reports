Summary

Kim opens the October 14th report with three major disclosures from the weekend. The first involves underground mining operations beneath Iraq that had been producing sulfur clouds over Baghdad. Kim reveals that both the Chinese - using prison labor - and the U.S. Military with Global Headquarters had been separately promised exclusive rights to the same tunnels by Iraqi and Iranian officials working with the Order of the Green Dragon. The two groups ended up gassing each other out, and as of the report all tunnels have been eliminated. Kim notes that all gold and oil in the Middle East was already leveraged to the financial system by 1992. The second disclosure covers a probability analysis that Deep State military operatives ran over the weekend. Kim and her team scored 99.8% chance of success without the Deep State, and 100% with them. The Deep State's odds without Kim came back at under 8% at time of report and falling - down to 3.98% by the time of recording. Their odds with the Order of the Dragon: 0%. Despite these numbers, the meetings continue. Kim also describes finding and dismantling two additional black magic AI hologram facilities - one under the Palace of Versailles and one under Germantown in Danbury, Connecticut, a Project Paperclip relocation site - as well as Pine Gap in Australia. The third and most entertaining disclosure involves Nathan Rothschild and the ANNA system. Kim explains that the Association of Numerical Numbering Agency in Belgium serves as the registry for all bonds, birth certificate bonds, and corporate numbers worldwide, feeding into a tax collection system called XN that routes 80% of all global taxes to the Order of the Dragon families. Kim's team found the full network of screens where these funds were being held - in Iran, the UK, China, St. Petersburg, Germany, Chicago, Kenya, Myanmar, the Netherlands, and Scotland - all accessible through Waddesdon Manor. When Nathan Rothschild attempted to declare war against Kim through this system to access the tax funds, Kim used the attack as a line to locate and eliminate the entire network. Rothschild called everyone he knew demanding to know who stole his money. Kim confirms it was her, states it was never real money without her allocation anyway, and wishes him luck keeping Rothschild Bank afloat.
