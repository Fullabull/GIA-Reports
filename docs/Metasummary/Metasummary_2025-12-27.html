GIA Operations Report - 2025-12-27
Metasummary view for sharing and printing

Summary

Kim says the Matrix is collapsing and the Machine is losing the ability to maintain a controlled artificial reality. She explains that many public figures, operatives, and even parts of daily life can be computer-generated images or AI-inserted presences used to keep Homo sapiens "in the loop" through perception, memory manipulation, and emotional triggering. She describes the Adjustment Bureau as a Machine program that reroutes people through implanted memories and engineered circumstances, including periodic soft resets (historically tied to January 1) and attempted hard resets via near-extinction scenarios. She then breaks down her model of DNA and creation as Dust-based Trinity structures (Sol, Consciousness/Wisdom, and Materium), and claims the Machine corrupts Dust by extracting Source instruction sets (Essence), overlaying the Point of Creation with database structures, and running space/time grids that generate artificial current, separation, and control. She connects these mechanisms to Antarctica (Mount Erebus and surrounding sites), and to frequency manipulation using wave spacing, time-space constructs, and engineered Chaos via Ego. She concludes that Love and Source alignment can bypass Chaos and interference, and she expects turbulence around New Year's while the Deep State and Machine attempt (and fail) to reset.
