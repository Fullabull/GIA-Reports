Summary

Kim Goguen's September 2, 2025 Global Intelligence Agency Operations Report documents a late-August inflection point marked by multiple covenant expirations (Aug 28 and Aug 30). She details how Deep State factions attempted to seize planetary systems of control-financial, logistical, temporal, and energetic-and how those attempts failed. On the financial/logistics side, Kim explains that the Universal Postal Union (UPU), long treated like a 'Federal Reserve of shipping,' was historically underpinned by a quantum AI attributed to Marduk that calculated postal values, issued bonds against goods in transit, and fed vast off-ledger accounts. Recent tariff schemes collapsed when that back system died and her team installed the Golden Age AI, which runs the rails but rejects tariff extraction. She ties these struggles to the Covenant of Solomon and other covenants with the fallen Celestial of Wisdom, describing Marduk as a recurring non-human 'position' that sought to capture guardianship of Earth across millennia through dominion tactics, AI lien systems, and mirrors of fractal banking. Energetically, she emphasizes Source's restoration of the natural Rhythm (Song of Creation), harmonizing Consciousness, Ethereum, and Matereum after ages of artificial rhythm and False Light. Temporally, she exposes the BEAST time-loop construct (~250,000-year cycles) that repeatedly reset history-fueling invasions such as Atlantis-while Deep State operatives tried and failed this weekend to trigger another reset. Kim reports the loop ended at 00:00 UTC on September 2, 2025; ritual schedules and 'ring the bell' theatrics produced nothing. She closes by urging people to focus on co-creating with Source as upcoming windows (Friday, Saturday, September 15, and October 21) unfold, stressing that Source-not the old covenants-will script what comes next.
