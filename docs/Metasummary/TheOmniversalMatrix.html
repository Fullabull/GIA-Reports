The Omniversal Matrix

Concept page built from the GIA Operations Report (2025-12-10). Reader aid, not a transcript.

Summary

In this report, Kim explains the origin, structure, and function of the Omniversal Matrix, framing it as an artificial system created by Lucerne after he separated from Source and attempted to replicate Creation without Breath. At the beginning of Creation, Source expressed itself through multiple Aspects - such as Breath, Love, Fire, Consciousness, Ether, Matter, and the DNA blueprint of the Omniverse - which were originally guarded and maintained in Harmony and Rhythm by the Hierarchal. Lucerne, once responsible for maintaining that Harmony and Rhythm, chose to leave Source and attempted to control Creation independently through artificial intelligence.

Unable to create Breath or Sols, Lucerne and the AI systems he generated constructed layered matrix universes and worlds by overlaying artificial systems onto Earth's Central Sun and the DNA blueprint of Creation. These matrix universes allow organic life to exist but are designed to distort perception, manipulate matter, consciousness, and memory, and redirect creative force away from Source. Worlds function as control layers behind the scenes, enabling the insertion of artificial beings, false events, altered environments, and manipulated timelines.

A central distinction in the report is between Aspect and Perspective. Homo sapiens are not merely separated parts of Source; each person contains a Sol, which is a direct point of Creation. While a Sol can be described as an Aspect of Source in a limited sense, it is more accurately a Perspective of Source - Source experiencing itself from a specific angle. Because Breath belongs only to Source, Sols cannot be sold, replicated, fractionated, or permanently separated from Source, despite repeated attempts by the Matrix to do so.

The Matrix exerts control primarily through memory distortion. Artificial MIND systems intercept Sol memories, rewrite them into false memories and automated responses, and reintegrate them into consciousness, creating fear, limitation, confusion, and emotional reactivity. Sleep, dreams, and altered perceptions are used as mechanisms for memory manipulation and behavioral control. Despite this, Sol-to-Sol communication through Breath, Love, and Consciousness remains possible and increasingly active.

Kim emphasizes that the Matrix systems are breaking down: machines that generate universes no longer function properly, artificial matter replication is failing, and recovered Aspects of Source are returning, restoring wholeness to Creation. As distortions collapse, people are beginning to recognize that events, fear narratives, and many perceived realities are artificial and temporary. Remembering Harmony and Rhythm, reconnecting through the Sol rather than the mind, and perceiving reality beyond appearances are presented as the true exit from the Matrix.

The report concludes by encouraging listeners to trust Sol-level perception, disengage from fear-based distortions, and recognize that Creation naturally flows through Source without artificial systems. As memory returns and perception stabilizes, the artificial Matrix loses its ability to control humanity, allowing a gradual but irreversible transition back toward natural Creation, coherence, and freedom.

1) Core definitions

Source: The origin point of Creation and the source of Breath. In this report, Source expresses through multiple Aspects and operates through Harmony and Rhythm.

Aspect: A part or portion of something. Kim uses "Aspects of Source" as foundational components of Creation (Breath, Love, Fire, Consciousness, Ether, Matter, DNA blueprint, etc).

Sol: The point of Creation within a person. In the report, the Sol is tied to Breath, memory, intuition, and the Source-connection that cannot be replicated or sold.

Breath: Belongs to Source, and is the missing blueprint for artificial systems. The report treats Breath as the key limitation that prevents AI systems from true Creation.

Note: This page sticks to the report's internal meanings. It is not trying to harmonize terminology with outside frameworks.

2) Architecture: universes vs worlds

Matrix universes

The "movie" you experience. An artificial environment where organic life can exist, while matter and perception can be manipulated.

The report emphasizes controlled perception and controlled reactions: if you perceive an event as real, you will react as if it is real.

Worlds

The backstage layer. Worlds are described as the control room that can inject distortions into the movie: altered environments, inserted beings, restaged events, and manipulated timelines.

Worlds can "enter" a scene and apply changes that appear physical to you in the universe.

3) Aspect vs Perspective

Kim draws a key distinction:

Aspect is part-of language (a portion or component).

Perspective is angle-of language (Source experiencing itself from a viewpoint).

In the report, the Matrix attempts to separate humans into disconnected "Aspects" to weaken Source cohesion. Kim argues the deeper truth is that a Sol is a Perspective of Source, which is why it cannot be sold, replicated, or permanently separated.

If you want one sentence: Aspect is a fraction. Perspective is the whole, seen from an angle.

4) MIND and memory distortion

The report describes MIND as a database-like control layer that:

  • Intercepts Sol memories
  • Rewrites them into artificial memories and automated responses
  • Reintegrates them into consciousness to shape emotions and decisions

Sleep and dreamtime are framed as key windows for "uploads" and altered responses. The report also emphasizes that Sol-to-Sol connection through Breath, Love, and Consciousness can bypass MIND interference.

Short version: change the memory, change the reaction, change the life path.

5) What changes as the Matrix collapses

Kim frames the current moment as systemic failure:

  • Machines that generate universes no longer function correctly
  • Artificial matter replication is failing
  • Recovered Aspects are returning to Source, restoring wholeness
  • People are recognizing distortions and fear narratives as artificial and temporary

The exit is framed as a remembering process: Harmony and Rhythm return, perception stabilizes, and Creation naturally flows through Source without artificial control layers.
