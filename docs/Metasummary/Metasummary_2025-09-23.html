Summary

Kim reports a decisive shift away from a wartime, debt-driven Financial System toward an asset-based, Gold-backed model fed by direct Source current (Gold Aether). Recent operations severed connections to Dark energy (loosh) sources, halted the annual loosh/soul auctions, and provoked emergency security responses. These operations also removed genetic-shareholder controls in banks and began converting systems toward an asset-backed model. The changes are cascading through financial, military, intelligence, media and internet systems, producing immediate loss of elite protections and structural reconfiguration.
