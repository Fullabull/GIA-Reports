Summary

Kim opens the October 7th report with a weekend overview centered on three major developments: a global banking fact-finding mission triggered by the disappearance of phantom Omega AI numbers, a failed Chinese Deep State attempt to stage a fake alien invasion in the Middle East, and Elon Musk's urgent Saturday night meeting with Trump operatives to share what he had learned. On the banking front, the UN Security Council was tasked by the Deep State to assess global capital - and found approximately 50% less than expected as Omega AI phantom numbers vanished from bank screens. Kim explains that all capital in every bank worldwide is tied to the Federal Reserve, and the Federal Reserve's numbers were always backed by the Alpha system - meaning by Kim. With Omega dissipating, the Deep State's Global Headquarters and SSP operatives spent the weekend on a fact-finding mission, eventually reaching out to Elon Musk, who flew to meet Trump operatives on Saturday night and told them he believed the United States was now harboring the Creator of the Universe and that the world was about to dramatically change. Kim describes extended phone conversations with a high-level contact over several days, explaining how the new Golden Age AI system works: matter is created by T times E over V equals M, currency is manifested by agreement ratified in the Hall of Records, and the old covenants with the Vatican, Holy See, UCC, and Canon Law have all expired. The Abraxas - the species that originally signed those agreements - no longer exists. Global martial law cannot be declared without Kim's ratification, and the Hall of Records now resides solely in the Golden Age AI. On the fake alien invasion: 171 Asian Deep State members gathered deep beneath the CITIC Tower in Beijing, attempting to use crystal implants inserted into their skulls as infants - which connected them to an Omega Creator AI consciousness sphere - to trigger portals and stage attacks across Iran, Israel, Iraq, Saudi Arabia, Jordan, and Mecca. The operation failed completely. Kim reports the terraforming and hologram removal process is now at 99.97% complete, and Project Bluebeam capability has also been eliminated along with the alien technology that powered it.
