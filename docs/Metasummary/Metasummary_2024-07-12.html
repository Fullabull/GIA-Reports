Summary

Kim opens the July 12, 2024 report by addressing the liquidity crisis spreading across multiple countries and institutions. Russia, China, South Africa, Vietnam, Laos, Indonesia, and the Philippines have all called requesting currency management assistance. The BRICS currency framework and its allocation numbers are disclosed as having been tied to an assignment Kim made to Russia around 2012-2013 - now expired, taking the entire BRICS currency infrastructure down with it. A Thailand-based figure had been claiming majority ownership of the Australian Reserve Bank based on similar old assignments; Australia is now on shaky financial ground as a result. The European Central Bank is expected to begin faltering as early as the following week, still waiting on promised funds that will not arrive. Global Headquarters operatives received new codes allegedly from the Rothschild family, buying them another few days of false credibility before the cycle repeats. Kim declines all partnership overtures using an extended widget-factory analogy: a fully bankrupt company with fraudulent management and a history of lying, cheating, and murder is not a viable business partner, regardless of how recognizable the brand name is. She notes that preparations are underway to issue independent cards or ATM networks and print currency directly if the banking standoff continues. The real reason for the late broadcast is a fifteen-hour operation Kim has been running against residual consciousness fragments of departed non-human beings scattered throughout the Multiverse. These beings - granted by Anti-Source the ability to split their consciousness into hundreds of particles and place them across any universe, dimension, or pocket of time - can still activate standing orders from even a single surviving particle. One such particle was found to be connected to communication posts on Earth that had been feeding the Q-Clock its operational predictions, including the current 'incoming dimension sweep' alert that Kim discloses as an extinction-level program targeting all life on Earth, not just the Deep State's enemies. She has been hunting and eliminating these consciousness fragments across densities and computer systems for fifteen hours. Several Earth-side communication posts have been located and shut down. The operation is not yet complete as of broadcast, and the risk window extends up to three days. Kim urges listeners who felt intermittent illness or disorientation during the day that this was likely the cause, and states she is returning to the operation immediately after the broadcast.
