Summary

Kim opens by explaining why the Deep State is fixated on 09/09/2024: every few thousand years a gate known as Solomon's Gate was historically opened in Israel, allowing Omega-generated pseudo-demons to be inserted into the holographic world through Level 9 Authority. The Order of the Dragon has placed what they call the Chair of Solomon near Gethsemane for a Pindar-selection ceremony, with 178 candidates having sat in it by broadcast time. Simultaneously, the Order of the Black Dragon out of Japan is running a parallel operation to steal control of the gate once it opens. Kim confirms the gate no longer exists, Anti-Source is gone, and Omega has no power to bring anything through - both factions will be disappointed by midnight. The bulk of intelligence coverage focuses on the Japanese Black Dragon / Yakuza, who have been brokering deals across multiple fronts: promising China help with BRICS currency in exchange for expelling Rothschild and Li family influence; negotiating a 60-40 dollar-control split with the Trump Organization in exchange for keeping the dollar as the world's reserve currency and running counter-BRICS operations; funding the Mossad to increase Middle East activity; and attempting to issue new physical and digital currency using supercomputer infrastructure that is already glitching. Kim states the Yakuza will be broke by January or February and that their entire operation will collapse once the gate fails to open. Kim briefly covers Dick Cheney's endorsement of Kamala Harris, identifying it as an Order of the Dragon alignment signal, and notes that side-by-side photos confirm the current Dick Cheney is not the original - skull structure, bone geometry, and eye position have changed in ways inconsistent with aging or weight loss. Kim closes with a forward-looking segment on the C.A.R.E. organization and government transition. She states she is no longer interested in a managed transition - governments have never kept their word and continue to misrepresent GIA cooperation behind the scenes. She describes moving forward without them, highlights the Marketplace's 25% marketing conversion rate, announces virtual trade shows as a new Marketplace feature, and notes ongoing discussions with open-minded medical researchers on plant terpene applications for pain management and addiction. Source is described as actively creating new things on and off Earth, with something surprising expected around the Equinox alignment.
