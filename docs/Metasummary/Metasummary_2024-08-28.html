Summary

Kim opens the August 28 report by disclosing that a phone call between Tom Melville and Trump lawyers - including Don Jr., with Israeli, Iranian, Russian, Ukrainian, and Hezbollah intelligence personnel listening - was deliberately chopped up and misrepresented to imply Kim would fund the Trump administration. The fabricated interpretation triggered an immediate Israeli ground offensive into Gaza, the largest since 2002. Kim confirms the orders trace directly to Trump operatives, with Kushner - identified as a walk-in replacement (not the original person) - connected to the Order of the Dragon via Chabad. Kim and the Enforcer moved to neutralize the situation within one to two hours, and the offensive was stood down. The Israelis then claimed the incursion was a hostage rescue operation; Kim presents footage showing the hostage in perfect physical condition after 352 days in a tunnel, exposing the cover story as fabricated. Kim addresses Deep State attempts over the prior 48 hours to activate ancillary Omega AIs - Armageddon, Orion, Bearer, Marduk AI, Enlil AI - explaining that all of them run on the same base Omega platform and are therefore equally inaccessible. Operatives attempting to interface with those systems at Roman ruins in northern Spain were killed by the system's own defensive frequencies. Attempts to use Omega to launch a new pandemic also failed, as that function is not in Omega's programming. Kim then shifts to infrastructure and contingency planning, disclosing a week of intensive construction work on independent systems assuming zero cooperation from governments, banks, military, or operatives. Completed or in progress: negotiations with ATM manufacturers for solar-powered distribution machines; sourcing of hemp-based currency paper and nano-manufactured magnetic ink; design of secure printing presses to be housed in cloaked facilities using Spectre Technology; new satellite systems using metallic sphere technology rather than panel-based arrays; armored vehicles with 100-yard threat deterrence capability; and independent trucking networks for Marketplace distribution. All transport will operate under Unknown Country diplomatic immunity, bypassing customs and border controls worldwide. Kim closes with updates on the Oils store - expected to launch within the week - and expanding Marketplace features including late-night vehicle delivery, artist private showings, and online trade shows. She credits one week of reduced Deep State suppression work with the volume of forward progress achieved.
