Summary

Kim opens the October 21st report covering a packed weekend. She begins with the BRICS peer-to-peer payment network launch, identifying it as a money laundering vehicle for Black Nobility and a surveillance backdoor - noting that Bitcoin and all blockchain are fully traceable via master nodes, confirmed by her own payment systems developers. Russian mafia arms flowing through Cyprus to Lebanon, Israel, Iran, and Sudan are detailed, with a Russian plane reportedly shot down over Sudan by Sudanese forces. Kim notes the Middle East war has become a money pit with no gates opened and no payments made, sustained only by Rothschild religious appeals to Israel about the Third Temple. The bulk of the report covers the ongoing SSP deception operation against their own allies. Langley 5 spent the weekend collecting debt numbers from militaries, secret societies, and financial institutions on the promise of system access by Friday at 5pm, then Saturday - none of which materialized. Kim reveals the SSP's actual plan was to crash the global financial system and buy everything back for pennies on the dollar - Marduk's original plan, which the Rothschilds received from Marduk and which the SSP is now trying to execute without him. This did not go over well when revealed in the Saturday meeting, where 22 people were eliminated by their own colleagues in anger. On the equinox deadline - the last day of which Kim notes falls October 21-22 - the SSP sent operatives to the Center of the World pyramid in Felicity California, the Grand Canyon SSP base, the Level 6 facility under the Pentagon, facilities in Kentucky and North Carolina, and the Giza pyramids, all looking for Abraxas arrivals or Omega system restoration. Kim joined one of the secret meetings uninvited, introduced herself as Ground Command, uploaded proof files showing all money always came from her not the SSP, and watched the reaction. She closes by confirming no loosh occurred, the debt call continues, and the SSP will be on to the next alignment date.
