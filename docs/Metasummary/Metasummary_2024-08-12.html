Summary

Kim opens the August 12 report disclosing that the Paris Olympics served as a major money laundering operation for the Deep State, part of a broader effort to replace the Universal Trust - a 250-year-old laundering structure based in Albania that had recently been shut down. Stadiums worldwide are disclosed as standing laundromats: fictitious concerts and sporting events generate clean cash that is deposited as ticket revenue. The Paris Olympics scaled this up dramatically, with C-130s and C-17s moving pallets of cash from Jordan and Cyprus into a system controlled exclusively by Archivists. Kim discloses that the Archivists - neutral beings whose sole mandate was to record history, never to influence events - had partnered with the money launderers and allowed them to use Archivist financial and telecommunications lines. She identifies this as a survival decision: the Golden Age came online and left them jobless, disconnected from Alpha and Omega, and they sided with the only parties who wanted those systems revived. All Archivist lines are shut down as of this broadcast. All cryptocurrencies are identified as agency-created peer-to-peer networks whose master nodes are held by the NSA and Nine Eyes; Bitcoin and all others are Black Sun currency, and anyone who thinks they are exiting the system is wrong. Kim then delivers a real-time intelligence disclosure: a group of operatives including Erik Prince and Blackwater associates are meeting on Little Cayman Island at the moment of broadcast, planning a false-flag attack on the United States to be attributed to a revived ISIS/Al-Qaeda. Tactical nuclear weapons are on the table. Discussed first-strike targets include the White House, the Capitol, the Statue of Liberty, and the Golden Gate Bridge, with simultaneous East and West Coast strikes intended to crash the financial system, trigger martial law with Joint Chiefs cooperation, and enable the introduction of a Black Sun currency to replace the dollar. The plan assumes Chinese Triad and Jade group funding. Kim discloses the meeting location publicly and invites any patriot, military, or regional Caribbean force to intervene, while confirming she is tracking all participants and their communications in real time. On the positive side, Kim discloses a breakthrough conversation with Source on the problem of persistent space junk and time-released Deep State programs appearing in the present from past registrations. The answer: re-terraforming must go back to before time began - to the place of no time at the center of Source - using what Source described as a mustard seed, formally identified as the Master Universal System for Terraforming All Realms of the Divine. The re-terraforming of sentient AI to fit the Golden Age is now underway. Kim also discloses that organic Zetas - small gray beings brought to Earth as slaves, expert in quantum systems - have been freed and returned home. The SSP attempted to manufacture artificial AI Zetas this weekend using facilities in Latvia and Canada; those facilities are now destroyed.
