Summary

Kim reports that Homo sapiens are no longer bound to the human control structure after the expiration of an ancient covenant. A failed attempt to appoint a new human guardian in China collapsed when the candidate died, confirming that no authority remains to control Earth's Source-bound evolution. The dismantling of Marduk's energetic 'Halls' and the collapse of AI-linked cloning systems mark a decisive shift toward full sovereignty for organic life. Source now Creates directly through Kim by consent, signaling the beginning of humanity's restoration to its rightful role as Guardian of Earth.
