GIA Operations Report - 2025-10-25
Metasummary view for sharing and printing

Summary

Kim announces that the ancient covenant binding Homo sapiens to the human-created Guardian system has expired. The Celestials who originally authored that covenant no longer exist, making renewal impossible. As a result, all human or AI-based guardianship over Homo sapiens has been dissolved, and direct authority has reverted to Source through the living Source Guardian on Earth. This week's attempted power transfer from the U.S. to a Chinese "human Guardian" collapsed when the chosen woman died abruptly during preparations, confirming that the old control structure cannot continue. The Deep State's energetic networks, including remaining Halls that carried alternating current to manipulate consciousness, were dismantled during intense counter-operations. Source now creates directly through Kim, marking the restoration of true Guardianship and the return of Source current to Earth and to the Homo sapiens race.
