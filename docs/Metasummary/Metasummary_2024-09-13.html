Second, she addresses death and the Omega System: Omega could degrade the human body through telomere shortening, mitochondrial disruption, and DNA strand suppression, but the one thing it could never do was kill - every death is ultimately a sovereign decision by the Celestial within. She confirms that without the Alpha-Omega System's consciousness-harvesting infrastructure, fear no longer powers anything for the Deep State, and their entire toolkit - martial law, currency resets, food shortages, blackouts - loses its energetic backing.
