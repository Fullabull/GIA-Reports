Summary

Kim delivers a comprehensive technical disclosure of the global financial system's architecture, presenting a live whiteboard briefing that traces both the backside (control) and frontside (banking) systems that governed all money creation, currency issuance, and financial transfers on Earth. Kim discloses that the backside system operated across nine levels, with Control and Command at Level 9 functioning as the ultimate authority over all financial platforms, intelligence and military systems, telecommunications, internet infrastructure, Stargate and StarNet communications, and MKUltra brainwave technology. The backside system's Screens 1 through 8 controlled everything from account identification and signatories through transfer protocols, Fed banker codes, and Family banker access - all of which required codes issued exclusively by the backside system. Kim confirms that her passport and credentials now appear on Screen 2 as the sole signatory, replacing all prior Family, Dragon, and Black Sun access. The frontside banking system is disclosed as a nine-level dependent structure built on the backside system's foundation, running through the NSA and Nine Eyes, CIA-controlled bank back offices, Oracle and Microsoft indexing systems, Pentagon firewall functions, the Federal Reserve, Family banker codes, and the BIS/IMF layer - before reaching the individual account holder at Level 1. The Gatekeeper program, formerly housed under Tel Aviv, served as the lock-and-key control mechanism requiring sequential code passage through all levels. Gatekeeper ceased functioning in December 2019, though its coding placeholders remain in the frontside system, requiring Kim to issue all access, key, data, batch, and compliance codes on a transaction-specific basis. Kim further discloses the hierarchical control structure above the financial system itself: Enki, Enlil, and Marduk occupied Level 9 above 21 Parents, 42 Covens, the World Council, Pindar, and the Families and Agencies below. The previous Level-9 guardians have been removed - Kim now holds Level 9. The Covens and World Council continue to ignore her authority and await instructions that will never come. Kim explains that the energy cycle underlying this structure reflects Tesla's triangle - 3, 6, 9 - and that the system was designed as the Ring of Solomon to extract human life-force energy in exchange for currency. She has begun reversing this, enabling her to push funds outward rather than requiring the old lock-and-key mechanism. Kim closes by confirming that all access, key, data, and family codes have been secured, that the Prometheus, Westland New Zealand, Chinese Elder, and similar systems cannot reintroduce siphoned funds, and that her immediate goal is direct transfer to end recipients without interference from the UN, IBM, HP, Microsoft, or Federal Reserve. She reaffirms that all of humanity - not the Families, not the Dragons - are the true beneficiaries of the Trust, and that those who held prior positions were always guardians only, never owners.
